The Cooling Face Mask Lady Gaga Uses De-Puffed My Tired Skin

If something is good enough for Lady Gaga, it’s good enough for me, especially a beauty product. I trust the pop icon wholeheartedly, and if her porcelain smooth skin is any indication of her smart skincare choices, then I’ll give anything she likes a whirl.

Lately, I’ve been on the go so much that I’ve barely given my skin any room to breathe — like wearing makeup from dawn until dusk almost every single day, thanks to New York Fashion Week. My skin savior through it all has been the Cryo De-Puffing Facial Mask from 111Skin. It soothed my swollen, tired skin and made me look refreshed enough to take on more events without looking like I pulled an all-nighter. 

111Skin Cryo De-Puffing Facial Mask 

Face masks, in my opinion, are the epitome of self-care. There’s nothing like relaxing while wearing a cooling face mask in the morning or at the end of a long day, specifically one with loads of skin benefits. Meant to mimic cryotherapy (i.e., chilling or icing your skin to tighten pores and reduce redness), this mask does just that by blending together caffeine to minimize dark circles and puffiness and a mix of peptides to prevent water retention like face bloating and promoting circulation. 

I deal with a good amount of puffiness right after I wake up, and it’s almost always around my eye area and T-zone, so I put the mask to the test after my morning coffee since I had several events I had to go to that day. The brand suggests leaving the two-piece mask on for 20 minutes. However, I left mine on for 15 and noticed an incredible improvement in my swelling and overall dull complexion. My eyes looked completely revived, and my skin looked dewy and moisturized. 

Lady Gaga — who is a fan of the de-puffing mask — used it as a pre-makeup treatment in tandem with an ice roller in a TikTok video. She then proceeded to apply full-coverage glam. I can confirm my makeup lasted all day after using this pre-makeup method of Gaga’s, and I will 100 percent be doing it again.
