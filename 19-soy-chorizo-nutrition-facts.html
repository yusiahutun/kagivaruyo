19 Soy Chorizo Nutrition Facts

Source: Thekitchn.com

When it comes to exploring plant-based alternatives to traditional meat products, soy chorizo is gaining popularity for its delicious taste and versatility. Soy chorizo, also known as soyrizo, is a plant-based version of the popular spicy Mexican sausage. Made primarily from soy protein, it offers a flavorful alternative to those who follow a vegetarian or vegan diet, or simply prefer to incorporate more plant-based options into their meals.
