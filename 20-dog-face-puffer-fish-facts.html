No, Dog Face Puffer Fish are not dangerous to humans. While they have sharp teeth and the ability to bite, they are generally docile and not aggressive towards humans.

Yes, Dog Face Puffer Fish require a well-maintained saltwater aquarium with suitable filtration and ample hiding places. They also need a varied diet including live and frozen foods.
