Tips for growing and maintaining short dreadlocks

Like with natural hair, your goal should be to achieve growth in volume and length and have healthy dreadlocks. Therefore, as you nurture the short locs, here are some tips to help you throughout your journey.

Ensure that the dreadlocks are residue-free

You can do this by keeping the locks tight at all time. When you wash with shampoo, rinse and dry thoroughly to avoid any remains. Residue causes scalp irritations and makes it hard for locks to dry.

Moisturize your dreads to avoid breakage

You do not want your hair to be falling or to have very thin dreads. Therefore, apart from regular moisturizing, watch what you eat. Proper nutrition is essential for healthy hair growth.
