4. Benjamin Wangari

About Inooro FM radio

Inooro FM is one of the most popular kikuyu vernacular stations in the country. It provides content that is not only suitable for adults but children as well. It covers several areas in the country. These places include all parts of Central region at 97.8 FM, Nairobi at 98.9FM, Mombasa at 99.2FM, Nakuru at 88.9FM, Chuka at 102.0FM, Meru at 95.1FM and Eldoret at 107.0FM. It also reaches other areas but the ones stated above are where majority of its fans are located.

Inooro fm awards

Currently, Inooro FM is one of the leading Kikuyu vernacular stations in the country. This is evidenced by the awards that the station got from this year’s Kuza Awards. It not only beat other stations that lead in the Kikuyu language but all vernacular stations in the country. It was awarded due to facilitating its services excellently. The station got a total of 5 awards in agriculture, women, youth and persons living with disability, children welfare and people’s choice awards. The station also received an award for being objective and impartial in presenting current affairs and news. Based on such recognitions on such a major event, it is quite clear that the station will achieve more in future. Each and every person who works there carries out the duties assigned to them diligently. We therefore can expect more awards for this station in future.
