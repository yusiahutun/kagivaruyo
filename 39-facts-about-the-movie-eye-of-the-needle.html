39 Facts about the movie Eye of the Needle

Source: Amazon.com

Released in 1981, “Eye of the Needle” is a gripping thriller film based on the bestselling novel of the same name by Ken Follett. Set during World War II, the movie tells the story of a ruthless German spy known as “The Needle” who possesses valuable information that could alter the outcome of the war. Directed by Richard Marquand and starring Donald Sutherland and Kate Nelligan, “Eye of the Needle” takes audiences on a suspenseful journey filled with espionage, romance, and high stakes. With its intricate plot, outstanding performances, and compelling storytelling, it’s no wonder that this film has become a classic in the spy genre.
