15 best AI Dungeon alternatives and similar online games

AI Dungeon is a single-player and multiplayer adventure game that uses artificial intelligence to generate content. The game has straightforward gameplay whereby players create and share their custom adventure settings. When you start playing the game, you get a screen with instructions. Then, you have to choose a mode relevant to the instructions given. But are there other AI Dungeon alternatives with the same gameplay features?

AI Dungeon gets updates from time to time. Recently, it released an update where the game developed a new system that stops it from generating sexual content involving minors. The update created the Ai Dungeon controversy, whereby players questioned its implementation.

The game forces players to use other prompts once they create explicit content about minors. While some justified the update, it made many players look for other games similar to AI Dungeon.

What is wrong with AI Dungeon?

AI Dungeon remains the best text adventure game. However, it does not generate quality and cohesive prose. Furthermore, the games do not adapt well to the writing styles of different players. Another downside of the game is that the stories generated are incoherent and garbled.

Is AI Dungeon still free? AI Dungeon is free to play. However, you have to create an account to enjoy more game features on your Android or iOS device. The free option has ads, but you can remove them through an in-app purchase.

1. Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead

Cataclysm: Dark Days Ahead is a turn-based survival game set in a post-apocalyptic world. It is one of the best free Ai Dungeon alternatives that use text and allows players to create a world that they can share between various characters with no end goal.

You get placed into one of many possible starting scenarios, and you are free to do whatever pleases you. Cataclysm has official builds for Windows, Linux, OSX, and Android.

2. Dreamily.AI

Dreamly.AI is an AI-assisted creative writing tool for creators and literary enthusiasts. It has many built-in collaborative writing models and allows you to set the number of words you want the AI to write. So if you find yourself struggling to write a story, Dreamly.AI will give you a boost whenever you get stuck. Dreamly.AI is available on Android devices.

3. Dwarf Fortress

Dwarf Fortress is a construction and management simulation video game. The freeware allows players to indirectly control a group of dwarves in a procedurally-generated fantasy world and try to construct a successful and wealthy fortress.

Like AI Dungeon, Dwarf Fortress has text-based graphics and an open-ended theme with no primary objectives. Dwarf Fortress is available for Windows, Mac, and Linux.

4. HyperWrite

HyperWrite is an online interface that uses natural language processing to enhance a person’s writing. It is worth it for people that know what they want to say but cannot figure out how to say it. It allows you to provide it with whatever you are looking for, and it will generate original paragraphs for you to use.

5. InferKit

InferKit provides a web interface and API for AI-based text generators. It gives you a platform where you can write anything, and then it generates a paragraph of what it thinks will come next. The application is configurable and will give you more text on any topic. You can use InferKit on your desktop or laptop.

6. Infinite Story

Infinite Story is an interactive fiction engine that lets you read and collaboratively write any story you want. You can choose from hundreds of topics provided and create things like interactive workflows, image adventures, and menu prototypes. The engine is available on a web interface, meaning you do not have to download an app to use it.

7. Magium

Magium is a text-based adventure game on mobile devices. It allows you to play an ordinary character trying to win a mage tournament against the most powerful mages. Your goal is to use the prize to fulfil the character’s dream of becoming a mage. Magium is the best Ai Dungeon alternative for Android and iOS devices.

8. NovelAI

NovelAI is a literature generation platform powered by artificial intelligence. It allows you to construct unique stories or do anything within or beyond your imagination. Once you write what is in your mind, NovelAi will seamlessly adapt to your input and maintain your perspective and style. But unlike AI Dungeon, NovelAI is not free. You require a monthly subscription to access its features.

9. Philosopher AI

Philosopher AI is a text-based interface that allows you to create anything imaginable. The platform gives you an AI-based engine that will generate text on different topics.

In other words, it will predict what you might come next after supplying it with a few words. Furthermore, you can use the engine on the latest browser without downloading any software or app.

10. Project Electric Sheep

Project Electric Sheep is an immersive dream simulation game. It gives you a platform to type anything you want and enter a visual interpretation of your dream world.

The game will create characters, places, and dialogue and adapt to what you supply it. Unfortunately, the game is only available on browsers. You also have to be a subscriber to use it.

11. ShortlyAI

ShortlyAI is a blank canvas AI-writing assistant with unlimited text generations. It uses artificial intelligence to make writing easier and faster for users.

12. TextSynth

TextSynth provides a platform that allows you to type your thoughts and generate paragraphs of text. It uses the latest technologies to create language models and parameters to evaluate your writing and provide meaningful text.

Once you supply it with a text, it will use the neural network to complete the paragraph. You can try as many times as possible, with each try returning a differently chosen completion.

13. Twine

Twine is an open-source application that gives you an application for telling interactive and nonlinear stories. Once you type a text in the interface provided, Twine will extend it with conditional logic, variables, and images.

14. Write Holo

Write Holo is a platform that allows you to organise your thoughts and write on any topic. It has datasets for every work imaginable, including novels, short stories, and fan fiction.

The platform has metadata UI that lets you tune its artificial intelligence technology to evoke from a myriad of different genres and authors. Write Holo is premium but offers a free trial.

15. Write With Transformer

Write With Transformer is a neural network that helps you auto-complete your writing. The web app allows you to write anything directly from your browser, and then it triggers a text to complete your work.

While Write With Transformer may not be closer to anything like AI Dungeon, the concept is the same since you can use it to continue a story or generate text in areas where you get stuck.
