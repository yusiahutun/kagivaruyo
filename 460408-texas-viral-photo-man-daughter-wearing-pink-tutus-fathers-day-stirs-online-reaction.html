Texas: Viral Photo of Man, Daughter Wearing Pink Tutus on Father's Day Stirs Reaction Online

  • A father has caused mixed reactions from netizens after sharing a photo with his girl wearing pink tutus on Father's Day
  • The photo did rounds on social media, with many sharing different views on the decision of the man
  • While some hailed the dad for taking a bold step, others criticised him, saying he was not manly enough

The internet can be an unforgiving place when someone puts out an opinion contrary to the beliefs of many.

A disputable photo of a man and his adorable daughter donning pink tutus has sparked a debate online.

Photo goes viral

Casey Field from Texas shared the picture with his one-year-old daughter, wearing similar outfits on Father's Day.
