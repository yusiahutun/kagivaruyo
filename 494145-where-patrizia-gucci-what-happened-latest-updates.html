Who is Patrizia Gucci?

Patrizia Gucci is an Italian convicted criminal and former socialite. She was born in a small town called Vignola, located in Modena province in Northern Italy. Named Patrizia Martinelli by her mother, a waitress, she grew up poor and didn't know her biological father.

When Patrizia was 12, her mother married Ferdinando Reggiani, who made his fortune in trucking. He adopted Patrizia and changed her last name to Reggiani.

How old is Patrizia Gucci today?

As of 2024, Patrizia Gucci's age is 75 years. She was born on 2nd December 1948.

Patrizia Gucci's marriage to Maurizio Gucci

In 1970, Patrizia met the grandson of Guccio Gucci, Maurizio Gucci, at a party. The duo married in 1972 and moved to New York. Although Gucci's father, Rodolfo, disapproved of the marriage, claiming his daughter-in-law was a social climber, he gifted them with a penthouse in New York city's Olympic Tower after the wedding.

Why did Patrizia Reggiani kill Maurizio Gucci?

One day in 1985, Maurizio took a business trip to Florence, Italy, but sent a friend the following day to tell his wife the marriage was over. After the separation, Gucci started dating his childhood friend, Paola Franchi, in 1990.

Patrizia and Maurizio's divorce was finalised in 1994. A year after their divorce, Patrizia hired a hitman, Benedetto Ceraulo, who shot and killed Maurizio Gucci at his private office Via Palestro 20 Milan on 27th March 1995.
