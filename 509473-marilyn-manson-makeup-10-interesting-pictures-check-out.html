Who is Marilyn Manson?

Marilyn Manson is a multitalented American rock musician loved and hated in equal measure. On top of being a singer and songwriter, Marilyn is a visual artist, actor, and record producer.

He came into the limelight with his controversial album, Antichrist Superstar. Marilyn is notorious for his “weird” way of life and antics, including his controversial stage personality.

Marilyn was born on January 5, 1969, in Canton, Ohio, in the United States of America. The rock musician got his name by combining the names of actress Marilyn Monroe and Criminal Charles Manson. Marilyn has even revealed that he trademarked his name.

Marilyn formed the band Marilyn Manson & the Spooky Kids along with guitarist Scott Putesky in 1989, and it has been a hit ever since, primarily because of the singer's controversies.

Manson also acts. He appeared in 1997 in David Lynch's Lost Highway and in 1999 in Rose McGowan’s movie, Jawbreaker.
