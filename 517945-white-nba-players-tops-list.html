20 best White NBA players right now: Who tops the list?

Ask any avid basketball fan to list the best white basketball player of all time, and they will always say Larry Bird. The former professional player set the bar so high that few white players compare. The current league also has great players. But who are the best white NBA players right now?

The NBA is an ever-evolving platform where athletes from diverse backgrounds showcase their skills, athleticism, and passion for basketball. Most of the NBA players are black, with the white guys in the NBA being the second highest, as Latinos and Asians follow.

The 6’5” tall Serbian professional basketball player was drafted in 2014 and has since had a fruitful career. Bogdan Bogdanovićfor plays for the Atlanta Hawks and his national team in Serbia. Before joining the NBA, Bogdan Bogdanović was an architect and urbanist in Serbia.

2. Kristaps Porzingis
