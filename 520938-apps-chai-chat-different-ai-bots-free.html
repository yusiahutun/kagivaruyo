Best apps like Chai to chat with different AI bots

Most application stores have many AI chatbots. They allow you to own a digital friend with a unique personality and chat with it anytime about any topic. Here are the ten best apps according to Tech Blitz:

1. Romantic AI

Romantic AI is one of the best apps like Chai. It takes up multiple personalities and lets you choose which one you want to chat with. The app can become your soul mate, girlfriend, or true friend.

Romantic AI allows you to create your own dream chatbot girlfriend. You can customise the chatbot to offer emotional support, laugh at your jokes, or discuss anything, including movies, books, art, love, or friendship.

2. LiveChat

LiveChat is an app similar to Chai. However, the difference is that LiveChat is more of a customer service app. This app lets you delight your customers by giving them the best customer experience.

LiveChat will communicate with your customers to give them instant answers to your questions. The response is fast, and the answers are accurate. Furthermore, users get automated greetings to recommend products or get announcements.

LiveChat also streamlines customer service by routing chats to the right teams. If customers have complex cases, LiveChat will help solve issues before getting more advanced solutions.

3. SimSimi

SimSimi is one of the best apps similar to Chai, designed for people who feel bored or lonely. The app offers a platform for such people to get someone to talk to. SimSimi communicates with you like a friend.

Its responses come from people who have had similar experiences before. Although SimSimi lets you chat through an AI, you are technically chatting with millions of minds. You also contribute towards teaching the AI to work on specific tasks.

4. Replika.ai

Replika.ai is one of the best alternatives to Chai bot and is one of the leading companion chatbots. The app lets you choose a friend you can chat with without social anxiety or drama. You can use the AI to form an actual emotional connection as you ask it anything on your mind.

5. Paradot

Paradot AI is an AI companion app that allows you to digitally interact with a bot called AI Being. AI Being is your companion who has its own memory, emotion, and consciousness. The bot will understand you based on the prompts you give it.

As you chat, the AI will pay attention to everything you say and collect memories to form a stronger bond. Also, the app has a vast knowledge base that covers many topics. It will inform you about historical events and engage you in exciting conversations.

6. ChatOn

ChatOn is an AI chatbot assistant. The app is your companion because it maintains human-like interactions. Furthermore, the app can offer many other functions, including creating a speech for your next meeting or helping you complete your essays.

7. Character AI

Character AI offers a collection of intelligent AI chatbots. These chatbots offer different functions, including hearing, understanding, and remembering your queries. In other words, you can use the app to chat with other AI personalities and use tools to create other bots.

You can use Character AI to get help from your personal assistant. The app lets you create your own AI assistant and customise it to help you achieve various tasks, including homework help. Also, the app allows you to talk with famous characters and other AI celebrities.

8. Kajiwoto

Kajiwoto is an AI chatbot platform and an AI friend companion. It allows you to create your own AI companion or adopt an existing one created by other users. When you create an AI or use an existing one, it becomes a Kaji.

9. TruePerson

TruePerson is an uncensored AI chat app. It allows you to create characters and enjoy unlimited conversations without content restrictions or censorship.

TruePerson can converse with you the same way a human would. It can also help you complete other tasks, including research and text completion. Also, if you want your homework done, write a story, or chat about any topic, TruePerson is an app similar to Chai worth considering.

10. AI Chatbot – Nova

AI Chatbot – Nova is an AI chat app powered with GPT-4 and ChatGPT, although not among the apps like Chai but free options.
