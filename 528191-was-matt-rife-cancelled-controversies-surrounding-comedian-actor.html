Early childhood 

The personality was born on 10th September 1995 in Columbus, Ohio, to the late Michael Eric Gutzke and April Rife Chilton. When he was 17 months old, his father's biological father killed himself, and he was adopted by his stepfather, Jason Siever.

The comedian grew up surrounded by four siblings, three older stepsisters and a younger half-sister. Growing up, his family relocated from place to place, and the comedian was raised in New Albany and Mount Vernon.

Rife dove into comedy at age 14 after encouragement from his peers to feature in a talent show hosted in his school. He then proceeded to mainstream comedy at age 15.
