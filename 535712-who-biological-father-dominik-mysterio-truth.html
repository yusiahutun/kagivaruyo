The story of Rey Mysterio, Eddie Guerrero, and Dominik Mysterio

Dominik Mysterio and Rey Mysterio first appeared together on WWE in 2005 during the feud between Rey and Eddie – he was eight. Eddie Guerrero blatantly told Dominik, “I’m your papi!”.

According to the "Who's Your Father?" storyline, Eddie Guerrero claimed he once had issues with his wife, Vickie Guerrero, and this made him cheat, resulting in the birth of his "son", Dominik.

Concurrently, his best friend Rey Mysterio and his wife Angie Mysterio struggled to conceive a child. In a surprising twist, Eddie gave them a generous offer to adopt his "son", Dominik.

So, a ladder match, Custody of Dominik, between Eddie and Rey, was scheduled for that year's Summerslam – the winner would allegedly be Dominik's father. Rey Mysterio defeated Eddie Guerrero.
