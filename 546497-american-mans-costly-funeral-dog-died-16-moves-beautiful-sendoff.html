American Man Holds Expensive Funeral for Beloved Dog, Cries Profusely At Burial: &quot;Beautiful Sendoff&quot;

  • Alex Holmes dug very deep in his pockets and accorded his beloved dog, Auitton, a funeral complete with mourners, a cleric, flowers and a casket
  • According to Holmes, his beloved dog loved wagging its tail at them, snuggling on the couch and playing fetch
  • Auitton brought immense joy and laughter into Alex's home and was also the co-owner of Flowerboy Productions

A cute dog that died after living for 16 years was accorded a beautiful funeral by its owner, who did not spare any expense to show how much he loved his faithful companion.

Auitton leaves behind heartbroken family

According to @delanahalo, who shared the video on Instagram, the funeral was a beautiful send-off for a pet, a service that often gets overlooked.

"Always remember, your pets are a part of your family!" he said.

Apart from Alex, Auitton sadly left behind a heartbroken father, Alex Holmes, grandma Debra, auntie Yawnie, cousin Princeton and many loving family friends.

"Auitton was a bright light in our lives, always greeting us with a wagging tail and puppy kisses. She loved playing fetch, going on walks, and snuggling on the couch. Auitton brought immense joy and laughter into our home as co-owner of Flowerboy Productions," said Alex. "She was a sweet, affectionate companion who will be deeply missed. Her loving spirit touched our hearts, and her memory will remain in our hearts forever," he added.

The family held the service for the cute dog and a pastor also spoke and comforted them. Dog lovers also attended the ceremony with their dogs and mounted the cute, departed animal.
