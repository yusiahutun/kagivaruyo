Judah &amp; The Lion Announce Pep Talk Tour

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Folk-rockers Judah & The Lion have a busy summer ahead of them with a new album and tour on tap.

The tour will be the band’s biggest run to date, kicking off at King Tut’s Wah Wah Hut in Glasgow on July 16th with dates scheduled across Europe through the rest of the month, including a gig at Lollapalooza Paris on July 24th.

JATL then heads to North America, starting on August 10th at the Starlight Amphitheatre in Kansas City, Mo with dates across North America through the Autumn when they close the tour out at the Coca-Cola Roxy in Atlanta on Nov. 8th.

However, before they officially hit the road, JATL is lined up for a showcase at SXSW on March 9th, as well as a post-game performance for the Milwaukee Admirals at Summerfest Stage on March 8th.

They are also booked for a small run of ‘intimate album listening parties’ at select North American clubs and theaters for their forthcoming album Pep Talk, which is scheduled to hit streaming services and what few shelves are left in May.

JATL are also lined up for festival plays this year that include Hangout, Wildfire, and the Forecastle Festival.
