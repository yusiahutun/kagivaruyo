The Butt-Flattering Jeans Sarah Jessica Parker and I Wear Are on Sale

It took me a while to actually like wearing jeans. The difficulties with denim started way back in middle school — you know, the time that low-rise jeans were a thing, and yes, I did own several pairs. I never really liked any of the blues I had, though; the wash wasn’t right, the fit wasn’t ideal for my curvy hips, and they simply weren’t comfy. So, I took a break from jeans for a while. Then, I started working in fashion and got more educated on all the denim brands out there (and hey, there are a lot). That said, few stand above 7 For All Mankind, a throwback label that I wore way back in the 2000s and still wear (almost every day) in 2023. 

7 For All Mankind is technically within the designer denim realm. The jeans are high quality (which you’ll see when you try your new pair), but that means they’re also pricey. However, you’re in luck: They're still on sale following Amazon’s October Prime Sale, and you can shop some of the brand’s most flattering styles for up to 63 percent off. Yes, now is the best time to try a pair to refresh your collection, and yes, I currently have all five of the below styles in my cart. No, I probably won’t buy them all (because I’m going on vacation this week and have to be more mindful of my spending), but I certainly will buy a pair.

Best 7 For All Mankind Jean Deals on Amazon

The label is clearly editor-approved (me! I approve!), but I’m not the only in-the-know fashion person who’s played favorites with the label. Sarah Jessica Parker repeatedly wears its styles, and if it’s good enough for SJP — the OG NYC style queen — you can rest assured it’s good enough for you. Reese Witherspoon is another A-lister fan, as are Gigi Hadid and Lucy Hale, proving the label is truly ageless, considering that celebs of, well, all ages have worn it.

There are actually quite a few styles on sale, but the ones I’m eyeing to make mine include these Jo bootcut jeans that feel very of-the-moment. They’re also stretchy — a must for me when I buy jeans — and high-waisted, which is the most flattering cut out there, in my opinion. 

I’m also totally into these boyfriend jeans because I like a low-key cool pair that feels nonchalant and unfussy — and the Josefina is the exact pair that Parker has worn on repeat. They have a loose, relaxed silhouette with a lived-in wash that just feels easygoing. Not to mention, they’re also made from stretchy denim that’s comfy but never over-stretches (I’ve definitely tested mine to the limits).
