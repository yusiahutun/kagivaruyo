Ariana Madix fails to win 'DWTS' Season 32 dance marathon

In the latest season, a new 'Monster-thon' was introduced, kicking off with a Hustle where the dancers remained within their designated areas.

Nine remaining couples donned costumes as vampires, werewolves, and skeletons to celebrate Halloween on the dance floor.

During the dance marathon, dancers had the freedom to move anywhere when the music transitioned to Charleston. The longer they stayed on the dance floor, the more points they earned.

Remarkably, the Dance Marathon's scores had the potential to shake up the leaderboard. Marvel star Xochitl Gomez secured victory in the season's inaugural dance marathon, tying for the top position on the leaderboard with Ariana Madix following their solo performances.

However, after falling short in the Dance Marathon, Ariana slipped down to the second position on the leaderboard by just a single point.

Ariana Madix gets emotional recalling 'most memorable year'

Ariana Madix selected the year 2023 as her 'most memorable year' due to the painful experience of discovering her ex-partner's affair.

She noted during the episode, "My boyfriend of nine years and one of my best gal friends betrayed me in ways that went so far beyond just cheating together that is so hard for me to even explain."

Ariana also disclosed that Tom Sandoval and Rachel 'Raquel' Leviss had begun their affair 'while I was at my grandma's funeral.' In the aftermath of this betrayal, Ariana revealed that she grappled with her mental well-being.

Nonetheless, she ultimately discovered a glimmer of hope amidst the heartbreak and channeled her emotional journey into her performance.
