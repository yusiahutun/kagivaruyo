Bella Thorne's high-fashion transformation! Actress debuts chic new style during Paris Fashion Week

Bella Thorne turned heads with her high-fashion transformation when she debuted several chic outfits during Paris Fashion Week.  

The actress and self once proclaimed 'wild child' appeared to have ditched her former green haired loud style in favour of a classier look as of late. 

Stepping out to attend the Haute Couture catwalk events, Bella, 26, wore a selection of refined, elegant ensembles, looking worlds away from her once clashing bold colours and revealing pieces. 

Having topped the worst-dressed list at the American Music Awards in 2016, with her ombré hair style, the former Disney Channel star is now choosing a more minimalist approach to her red carpet glam. 

Earlier this week, Bella cut a classy figure as she joined stars at Schiaparelli's Spring Summer 2024 show in Paris.

The public have seen Bella go from Disney darling to a wild child, before reinventing herself once again. 

In 2018, she insisted that giving fans a glimpse of her 'grittier side' was all about maintaining the freedom that she didn't have when she was a child star. 

The former Disney Channel star opened up about abandoning her cookie-cutter image in the documentary Inside The Life of Bella Thorne, which was released by Vogue.

Bella pictured in April 2023 as she showcased her sparky personality in snaps as she posed with her tongue out

Bella is known for switching up her look as she switches between rockstar chic and elegant dresses 

Bella explained in the behind-the-scenes video that her agents wanted her to go from 'Disney to comedy to lead love interest comedy to like Rachel McAdams type vibe,' which didn't represent who she is as a person. 

'I love Rachel McAdams, but I'm not Rachel McAdams,' she said. 'I like the darker things. I like the gritty things. I like movies that feel uncomfortable, because that is what life is. 

'It's why I go out of my way to do the s**t that I do that people are like, oh that's crazy or that's whoa, that's this. Well, that's me,' she added. 

When fans first saw Bella on TV, she was starring alongside Zendaya on Disney show Shake It Up, which aired from 2010 to 2013. However, she admited that she never had a desire to play CeCe Jones on the series. 

'I remember walking into that audition and it wasn't a choice. It was this pays the bills. This is what you do,' she recalls.

Bella's sister Dani Thorne explained that her younger sibling 'wasn't free before' when she was a teen star.

'She had a lot of contracts, and she had my mom and everybody trying to be like, "You have to be like this served on a platter," kind of thing,' she recalled.

Bella said that she was constantly being told to dress and speak in a certain way when she was growing up as a child star 

Bella agrees that she was constantly being told to dress and speak in a certain way. When she got older, she was terrified about the media finding out about her romantic relationships. However, after she turned 18, she realized it didn't matter.

'I was kissing my boyfriend and someone was video recording us, and I was like "Hey, come up to me and ask for a photo. But I can't have this out. Something like this could be very, very bad for me,"' she said. 

'People could say, "How could you do that, you whore? How could you kiss your boyfriend in a coffee shop?" And I take this phone in my hand, and I literally look at it. I am going to delete the photo, and I look at her and I'm like, "I'm 18. Have it!"'

That moment made her realize that she can do what she wants, which has led her to make it a point to reveal her 'grittier side.' 

Bella revealed she received backlash and had been dubbed a 'wild child' because she smoked marijuana and came out as a bisexual, but she believed she is held to an 'unfair standard.'

'100 per cent of everything I do is like, honestly, not that big of a deal. It's hard to be yourself,' she explains. 'I like to switch it up.'

Bella, who rose to fame as CeCe Jones in Shake It Up from 2010 to 2013, was 16 when the show aired for the last time.
