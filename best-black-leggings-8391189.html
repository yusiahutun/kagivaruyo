The 16 Best Black Leggings of 2024

A timeless little black dress is the ultimate, stylish piece that works for every occasion. The same is true for leggings: A great pair of black leggings can anchor your wardrobe and be dressed up or down. The right styles can take you from the gym to the office, and even out to dinner. Finding the perfect pair is easier said than done, though.

When shopping for a pair, there’s a lot to consider — opacity, fit, material, and length are just the beginning. Quality is also important because you’ll want to invest in a piece that can last through years of wear and washing without fading or pilling. We’ve scoured our closets and the market, consulting fashion and fitness experts to compile the ultimate list of the best black leggings.

What to Keep in Mind

Opacity

Opacity refers to how thick your leggings are. If they’re too thin, they won’t provide enough coverage for working out or warmth in cold temperatures. Thicker fabrics also have a dressier look that’s ideal for more polished outfits, especially in the workplace. When trying leggings on, make sure to squat or bend over to test their opacity. If you can see the color or pattern of your underwear through the fabric, we recommend steering clear.

Length

The length you select for your black leggings depends on a few things, but it’s mostly up to your personal preference. Capri pairs hit at the middle of your calf, ankle-length styles hit right above your ankle, and full-length leggings hit at or below your ankle. Some leggings (like the Lululemon Wunder Train High-Rise Tight) even come in all of these lengths, so you can stock up on your favorite pairs. Ultimately, finding the right length comes down to what’s most comfortable for you, what the weather outside is like, and how tall you are.

Your Questions, Answered

Can black leggings look work-appropriate?

Yes — as long as you select the right pair. Arad says the material is the most important thing: “A work legging should be a thicker material with a matte finish.” (Our favorite is the Cuyana Ponte Legging Pant.) “A thin or shiny legging should stay in the gym or yoga studio,” Arad adds. She suggests styling black leggings as you would cigarette pants, picking an elegant top, and playing with shoes and accessories that you’d typically wear to work.

How can you tell if a pair of black leggings is high-quality?

“The best way to tell if a pair of leggings is quality is not by price,” Arad says. She recommends touching the fabric in a store if you can. “Spread the fabric. If you can see your fingers, that’s a bad sign,” she notes.

How do you style black leggings?

“Proportions are key,” Arad explains. “Because leggings are tight, they should be paired with something oversized on top.” To dress them up for work, she recommends pairing your leggings with an oversized button-down shirt, chunky loafers, your go-to jewelry, and a structured bag. In cooler temperatures, she suggests a thick, knit sweater or a sharp blazer, plus your favorite boots. “Throw a trench over that and you’ll be elevating your leggings game times ten.”
