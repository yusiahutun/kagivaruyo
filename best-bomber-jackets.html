The best bomber jackets to buy now for 2024

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Christian Vierig//Getty Images

Loewe

This bomber jacket from Loewe features a leather trim and a puffy silhouette, making it a perfect transitional jacket for the UK weather. Team with your black tailored trousers and loafers for a low-key look.

The Frankie Shop

The Frankie Shop

This faux-leather style from The Frankie Shop is super cropped, meaning it will look great teamed with a very on-trend of high-waisted trousers.

Loro Piana

Loro Piana

No one does high-end basics quite like Loro Piana, so if you want to invest in a bomber that you will truly wear forever, this is the one for you. A super relaxed silhouette, the jacket is also reversible, meaning you can wear it with either cashmere or silk facing out.

Nina Ricci

Nina Ricci

Bomber jackets have a reputation for being casual, but they don't have to be. This satin style from Nina Ricci is case in point. It even features beautiful bow detail, and can be styled with a matching skirt.

Prada

Prada's loose-fitting bomber jacket comes in a beautiful silk fabric, making it perfect for throwing on over dresses during the summer months.

Bottega Veneta

Bottega Veneta

We love this utilitarian take on the bomber from Bottega Veneta with statement buttons up the front and a sharp collar. It would look great layered over a white shirt.

Cos

Another great style for transitioning through the seasons, this design by Cos will keep you warm with its pillowy quilting, which is made from 100 per cent recycled fabric.

Wardrobe NYC

Wardrobe NYC

We always turn to Wardrobe NYC for elevated capsule classics, and this khaki bomber is one of our favourite pieces from the brand this season. Reversible, it can be worn with a quilted finish too.

Studio Nicholson

Studio Nicholson

This bomber jacket from Studio Nicholson swaps in a silk finish for nylon, giving it a more relaxed feel. It would look great worn open and oversized with jeans and sandals.

Arket

This wool bomber from Arket is a great workwear option because of its dark hue and and crisp silhouette.
