8 chic garden mirrors to jazz up your outdoor space

A garden mirror will magically make your outdoor space look bigger, instantly! Shop the best garden mirrors in stock.

    Outdoor/indoor living is one of the biggest garden trends at the moment, and that's why garden mirrors are now so popular. From outdoor rugs to patio heaters, hammocks and even lamp-style LED garden lighting, we're shopping for garden decorations and accessories like never before, and a garden mirror is an absolute must.

    Mirrors in your garden garden can transform a cold, unwelcoming space into something so cosy. Use a garden mirror like you would in your home or think of them like a window or doorway – they can reflect light and add depth to your outdoor space, whatever its size.

    Where should a mirror be placed in the garden?

    Garden mirrors are usually fixed to a garden wall, but larger mirrors for the garden can be jauntily leaned against a wall or side of a shed. There are smaller garden mirrors available too, attached to plant plots for a decorative addition to a wall, or mini garden mirrors with shutters for that Italian garden feel.

    All are made from weather-friendly materials, to stop any tarnishing, rusting or weather damage. From garden mirrors with shutters to round mirrors, gothic-style and mid-century designs, these are our favourites.
