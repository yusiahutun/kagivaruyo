Do hair straightening products work for all hair types?

"Someone with fine hair or even someone with very curly hair should always keep in mind to protect your hair," Kimble says. "If you want to maintain healthy and beautiful curls, you must protect them. Lastly, these products will help prevent over-straightening hair to make it smooth."

17

How often should you use a hair straightening product?

Unless your hair is super, super fine—you could probably benefit from using these products every day. "You should use protectant serums, oils, or leave-in styling creams in daily for the best results especially when using thermal styling tools," Hillier says. If your product of choice is also a bond-builder like Olaplex, there's more you should keep in mind. "Olaplex products cannot be overused so it’s important to add them to your daily routine for optimal hair care and health," she says. "Be sure to apply enough to get the job done but keep in mind a little goes a long way since these formulas are highly concentrated."

How can you get straight hair really fast?

"A few tips I have for getting straight hair really fast is using the right shampoo and conditioner to help smooth your hair," Kimble says. "Definitely dry your hair with a towel first really well to get as much moisture out as possible."
