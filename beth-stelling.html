    Beth Stelling Comedian

    Stelling has performed on Jimmy Kimmel Live!, The Meltdown with Jonah and Kumail, @midnight, Last Call with Carson Daly, The UCB Show, The Pete Holmes Show and We Can Fix You. She has a Comedy Central Half Hour special released with her latest comedy album Simply the Beth available on iTunes and Amazon. Vulture named it one of the best comedy specials of 2015.

    Beth is also in Showtime’s special “Comedy of SXSW.” Beth tours stand-up comedy clubs, colleges, and festivals, guest-starred on the Amazon series Red Oaks, and wrote on Crashing, an HBO series from Judd Apatow starring Pete Holmes.

    Beth performed standup on Jimmy Kimmel Live after the host personally approached her after a performance she did at the Upright Citizens Brigade in Los Angeles. She has headlined the Upright Citizen’s Brigade show Church and opened for numerous renowned comedians like Sarah Silverman, Patton Oswalt, and Rob Delaney.
