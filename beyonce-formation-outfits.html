Here's How Much Beyonc's 'Formation' Wardrobe Cost

A few weeks ago, Beyoncé released a little music video for her new song "Formation"—maybe you've heard of it? 

Naturally, it took the Internet by storm, as all things Beyoncé-related do. Mixed in with the buzz about race and Red Lobster lyrics was the obvious (for us, anyway) commentary on Queen Bey's amazing wardrobe throughout the video. We're still not over how hard she slayed every. single. look. So we're revisiting each outfit, ID-ing the pieces, and adding up the price tags.
