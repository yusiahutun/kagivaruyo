Bianca Censori's has husband Kanye West's teeth as her phone wallpaper

Bianca Censori has been spotted with a new wallpaper on her phone, supporting Kanye West’s new teeth.

Kanye West and his wife have been in the spotlight many times over the past year. The celebrity couple was first spotted out together in January last year and is said to have tied the knot a month earlier in December 2022. Kanye, 46, had just divorced reality TV star Kim Kardashian, 43, after a very long and public separation. He and Kim were married for six years and have four children: North, 10, Saint, 7, Chicago, 5, and Psalm, 4.

Bianca Censori’s new phone wallpaper features Kanye West’s mouth

29-year-old Bianca was spotted out and about with her phone on display, and fans of the music star were quick to point out the picture of Kanye West’s new teeth.

But it must be the way the Kim K lookalike and Kanye share their love, as Kanye has also been seen with pictures of Bianca on his phone as wallpaper in the last few months.

Kanye’s new dentures cost him $850k

Kanye West debuted his new teeth on an Instagram Story last week. His titanium grills apparently cost an eye-watering $850k, but are also said to be fake. Instead of having his real teeth removed, he underwent a procedure called fixed prosthodontics which sees the dentures fixed onto a person’s actual teeth.

The dentist who performed the procedure told Complex: “He did not have his teeth removed. He still has a full dentition. Healthy and happy.”

The surgery seems to have been inspired by the movie villain Jaws (Richard Kiel). The James Bond villain first appeared in the 1977 installment of the spy franchise, The Spy Who Loved Me, and has made numerous appearances since.

Kanye shared a picture of them side-by-side suggesting the baddie was the inspiration behind the quite drastic move.

Fans react to the new grills

Taking to X (formerly Twitter) fans were quick to share their thoughts on Kanye West’s new teeth amid Bianca’s new wallpaper.

Someone else joked: “I see #kanyewest has got new teeth. What’s he do brush them with Brasso? [three laughing emojis]. Fml.”
