Ed Hartwell and Keisha Knight Pulliam have been an exclusive story in the entertainment sector for days now. So, are the allegations made by Ed on Keisha’s unborn child to be not his true? Is Big Tigger really the father of the child? Learn in detail about the whole matter.

Divorced With the Mother of an Unborn Child:

Big Tigger was not even connected to the split of Hartwell and Pulliam when the news first broke out that they were getting divorced. The ex-NFL star stated the split from his wife of just 6 months due to irreconcilable differences. It had only been a week that they had opened up about having a baby. He had also raised doubt on if the unborn child was his.

Tigger Suspected as the Father:

It was only later that there was a suspicion that the factor of divorce could be a radio personality, Big Tigger also the ex of the former ‘Cosby Show’ star. They had dated for 3 years until 2015, but the radio/TV host clarified himself on his show saying,

“There’s been lots of conversation and innuendo suggesting that I might be the father of someone’s child. There is... zero percent chance that the child that is being alleged might be mine, is mine.”

Big Tigger's Short Bio:

Big Tigger was born on December 22, 1972, in The Bronx, New York, the USA as Darian Morgan. He grew up in Co-op City where he played basketball for the Wheaton high school. He attended the University of Maryland, College Park where he interned at Washington DC radio station WPGC 95.5 FM. He has a staggering net worth of an estimated amount of about 2.5 million dollars. He was lately seen in Like a Boss (TV Series)  this year.
