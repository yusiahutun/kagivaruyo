Brooks Darnell Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth, Wiki

Brooks Darnell is a new sensation in the acting industry who is going to dazzle the audience in the new show called “Game of Love”. The news also erupted that he is being signed for a multi-picture overall deal with Hallmark Media.

Lisa Hamilton Daly, Executive Vice President, Programming, Hallmark Media said that Brooks Darnell is a triplet threat- he is an actor, singer, and musician- and Hallmark is delighted to have him for the next movie Holiday Heritage (released 16th December 2022). This movie was going to be the first-ever movie celebrating Kwanzaa. 

Hallmark Channel is going to debut a romantic new movie this Saturday, 11th March 2023 with a familiar face to Opera Soap viewers, none other than, Brooks Darnell. The handsome actor is starring opposite Kimberley Sustad in the new series, Game of Love.

Early Life

Brooks Darnell was born on the 26th of July 1982 in Warren, Ohio. He is the eldest of three sons born to his father and mother (names not known). His father named him after the name of baseball great Brooks Robinson. As per the sources, it is revealed that his mother’s name is Doreen Rodgers. Brooks Darnell had a great passion for performing at an early stage.

It was his mother who would push him to perform and dance to the hits of Michael Jackson and MC Hammer. He was scouted at the age of 16 at a local fair and was signed by a talent agency. Soon after he appeared at the IMTA talent showcase, New York City competition. He earned the position of being in third place out of four hundred monologue contestants. Eventually, he won entry to study at the prestigious Conservatory theatre school in San Francisco.

How did he enter into acting? 

Call it a sign of the universe, or something else, Brooks was thinking of switching his career to acting and he had to end his sports career that he had for the last two years, due to a sports injury. Again, Brooks began working on his acting career and the practice of working in front of cameras. Back in 2001, he was signed by the BMG Models in Chicago.

Subsequently, he became the first African American male personality to grace the firm. Next, he moved to Toronto and began studying under some most respectable acting coaches like Larry Moss and David Rotenberg. Now, as an emerging star in Hollywood, he is the most talented and the most charming personality on the rise, working with Hallmark Media Network.

Brooks Darnell Career

Brooks Darnell began working with Hallmark Media Network back in 2016 with the debut movie – All Yours. After that, he acted in 2019’s movie, A Christmas Miracle, by Hallmark Movies & Mysteries. Back in 2022, he was seen acting a featured role in the Hallmark Channel original movies Advice to Love By and A Winter Getaway. He also had the featured role of Dr. Nate Hastings on The Young and the Restless.

Back in 2012, Brooks Darnell had a feature film called Total Recall, a recurring role in Freeform’s Shadowhunters, and a guest appearance in some of the episodes of Bones, Breakout Kings, and Nikita. He is an emerging star of Hollywood who has achieved great success with the association of Hallmark Media Network. He is represented by Jason Barrett and Allan Grifka of Alchemy Entertainment.
