Boots And Hearts Festival Unveils Full 2024 Lineup With Jason Aldean, Matchbox Twenty, Jess Moskaluk

ORO-MEDONTE, ON (CelebrityAccess) – Republic Live, the powerhouse promoter behind the internationally acclaimed Boots and Hearts Music Festival, has unveiled the full lineup for the 2024 edition, promising another unforgettable experience for music enthusiasts. Taking place from August 8 to 11 at Burl’s Creek Event Grounds in Oro-Medonte, the festival is set to showcase a stellar lineup of rising talents from Canada and the US. On Friday (August 9), the Main Stage will host Boots first-timers Cody Johnson, Brothers Osborne, and Megan Moroney, alongside Canadian Country Music Awards (CCMA) and JUNO Award winners Jess Moskaluke and emerging artist Nate Haller.
