Michael Swier And Live Nation Create Mercury East Presents

published on June 12, 2024
NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Live Nation Entertainment today announced a new entity, Mercury East Presents, created in partnership with Michael Swier, co-owner of Bowery Ballroom and Mercury Lounge. The new promotions and booking venture, Mercury East Presents, will focus on live music in New York City. The partnership will create a network of independently-owned and operated venues including Mercury Lounge and Bowery Ballroom, Live Nation’s Irving Plaza, Gramercy Theatre, Warsaw and Ford Amphitheater at Coney Island Boardwalk with more to be added.
