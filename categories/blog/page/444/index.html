NBA stars Myles Powell, Andrew Nicholson sign with Bay Area Dragons as East Asia Super League Season

published on February 26, 2024
The Bay Area Dragons have bolstered their roster ahead of the East Asia Super League season, signing former NBA players Andrew Nicholson and Myles Powell. Nicholson, a Canadian forward, who spent five seasons in the NBA with the Orlando Magic, Washington Wizards and Brooklyn Nets joins Powell, a former Philadelphia 76ers guard, as the second import player to debut in the upcoming season. “We have signed a stand-out young scoring guard who has had a sensational college career and worked his way up from the G-League to the NBA,” Bay Area Dragons head coach Brian Goorjian said. What's the Point of a Facial Without Extractions?

published on February 26, 2024
Question: What's the point of a facial that doesn't include extractions? Isn't cleaning your pores the main goal? Answer: For younger patients with oily/combination skin, extractions may be a top priority, but there are lots of skin types for which extractions are too aggressive or unnecessary altogether. "Clients that have mature skin and are looking for firming, lifting and anti-wrinkle benefits, or clients with dry or sensitive skin, do not require them," says Liza Wong, master esthetician and founder of Elite Skin Care in San Francisco.
