Chantel Jeffries is a bronzed beauty as she leads stars at 2023 Pegasus World Cup

Chantel Jeffries was among the stars to attend this year's Pegasus World Cup, which was held at Gulfstream Park in Hallandale, Florida on Saturday.

The 30-year-old DJ/influencer - who shows off her trendy style on Instagram - posed up in a fitted patterned button-up top. She styled her upper half with matching flared trousers that hugged her slender legs.

Numerous other figures from the entertainment industry, like TV personality Amanda Kloots and musician Maluma, made appearances at the star-studded horse race, which began in 2017.
