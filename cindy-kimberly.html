Cindy Kimberly Bio, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Cindy Kimberly is a Dutch-Spanish model, social media influencer, YouTuber, and entrepreneur. She gained worldwide attention after Justin Bieber shared her photo on his Instagram account in 2015 and asked, “Omg, who is this!!!”.

Since then, she has amassed over seven million followers on Instagram, where she showcases her beauty, fashion, and lifestyle. She has also appeared on the cover of magazines such as GQ Spain and Sports Illustrated.

Early Life

Cindy Kimberly was born on 16th November 1998 in Amsterdam, Netherlands. She is of mixed ethnicity; her father is Indonesian, and her mother is Spanish. She moved to Spain with her mother when she was three years old and grew up in Costa Blanca. She attended Laude Lady Elizabeth School in Benitachell and later studied fine arts at the University of Alicante.

Kimberly has been interested in art and photography since she was a child. She also loved music and learned to play the piano and the guitar. She was a fan of Justin Bieber and ran a fan account dedicated to him on Instagram. She also posted selfies and drawings of herself on her personal account, which had around 150 thousand followers before Bieber noticed her.

Cindy Kimberly Career

Cindy’s career took off after Justin Bieber shared her photo with his millions of fans in December 2015. She became an overnight sensation and received offers from modeling agencies and brands. She quit her job as a babysitter, which paid her six euros per hour, and signed with Uno Models in Barcelona and Madrid.

She made her runway debut at Madrid Fashion Week in 2016, walking for designers such as Dolores Cortés and Ángel Schlesser. She also landed campaigns for brands such as Very.co.uk, Pull&Bear, Maybelline, Bershka, Stradivarius, and JD Sports. She graced the cover of GQ Spain in 2018 and Sports Illustrated in 2022.

Kimberly also ventured into YouTube, where she created a channel called Cindy Kimberly in 2016. She uploads videos about beauty, fashion, travel, vlogs, challenges, and Q&A sessions. She has over one million subscribers on YouTube as of July 2023.

Kimberly is also an entrepreneur who launched her own clothing line called Loba in 2020. The brand offers streetwear-inspired apparel and accessories for women. Kimberly designs the products herself and promotes them on her social media platforms.
