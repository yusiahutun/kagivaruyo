If there’s one New Year’s resolution that makes everybody’s list each year, it’s to exercise more frequently. But if you often find it difficult to stick with your goal for long, you may not be exploring all your options—that’s where fitness memberships like ClassPass can be of help.

ClassPass offers worldwide access to thousands of top-rated gyms, fitness studios and even salons and spas (yes, you can book a facial through the app). It’s the best way to get the most experience for your money since you can try out a variety of in-person classes weekly, from pilates and boxing to popular options like Solidcore and SoulCycle.

Best of all, you can tailor your membership to how active you’d like to be; opt for a 23 credit, 43 credit or 89 credit plan. Each class ranges anywhere from 5 to 14 credits, so you can mix and match your exercise routine based on where you’d like to spend the most time on a given week.

If you’d like to slowly dip your toes into the world of ClassPass, the fitness membership offers a one month free trial of 43 credits—you’ll pay $0 to start right now. 

Keep reading to find out how you can take advantage of this offer and make upping your fitness regimen one resolution you can be proud of in 2024.

Where can I use ClassPass credits?

ClassPass offers access to countless classes in your given area; options will vary depending on where you’re based. For a full breakdown of what is available in your city or hometown, check out this list here. Some examples include kickboxing, restorative yoga, Zumba, strength training, cycling and more.

How can I opt into ClassPass after the free trial ends?

ClassPass will remind you of your renewal terms two days before the free trial ends, after which you can either opt into the plan of your choosing or cancel your membership altogether.

Can I add more ClassPass credits whenever I please?

If you’ve opted into a paid membership and run out of credits before the next renewal cycle, you can always add more to access additional classes. Just go to the “Add credits” tab in the app to make your purchase.

Can I cancel my ClassPass membership at any time?

If you choose to leave ClassPass down the line after opting into a paid membership, the cancellation will need to be requested at least 24 hours prior to your next cycle date.
