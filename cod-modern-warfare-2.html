FTac Ripper 56 | Underbarrel in Warzone, MW3 and MW2

The FTac Ripper 56 is a weapon attachment featured in Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3 (2023), Modern Warfare 2 (2022), and Call of Duty: Warzone.

It is one of the Underbarrel attachments available in MW3 and MW2.

The FTac Ripper 56 is a Weapons Platform Specific Attachment, which means it is only usable within its specific Weapons Platform.
