Colin Farrell Flaunts Shirtless Physique After Gaining (and Then Losing) Weight for 'True Detective'

Skinny Colin Farrell is back!

The "True Detective" star put his fit physique on display on the streets of West Hollywood on Wednesday, as he walked around sans shirt following an intense yoga class.

Sporting a goatee, freshly-cut locks and track pants, Colin's appearance was a far cry from his current role as the paunch-packing Ray Velcoro on the HBO crime series.

The actor had to gain over 40 pounds for his work on both "True Detective" and upcoming film "The Lobster" -- something he wasn't too excited about.

"People think it must be fun to eat what you want - and it is, for the first day," he explained to Glamour. "Day two, when you're already on your second cheeseburger and fries with a chocolate shake by 11am, not so much."

"I do prefer to feel a little less slovenly," he added. "I usually work out a bit more - I do yoga and go hiking. But my grooming 'routine' mainly involves a tube of toothpaste and most days it stops there. I'm very low maintenance."

Clearly, Farrell got right back into his workout regimen -- and has the stomach to show for it!
