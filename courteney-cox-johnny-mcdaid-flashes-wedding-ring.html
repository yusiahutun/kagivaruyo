Courteney Cox's fianc Johnny McDaid sparks wedding talk with new ring

    Are Courteney Cox and Johnny McDaid married? The rumour mill was sent into overdrive when Johnny, 38, was spotted wearing what looked to be a wedding band on his ring finger on Sunday. Reports soon emerged that Courteney and Johnny had tied the knot in secret. The pair couldn't have looked sweeter as they enjoyed a romantic stroll in New York City yesterday.

    Courteney Cox and Johnny McDaid were said to be having two wedding ceremonies

    Dressed casually for their walk, Courtney, 50, had the look of love on her face as she held her beau's hand and cuddled up to him. The former star, who announced her engagement to Johnny after six months of dating in June, was pictured in a tender embrace with her rocker man at another point on their outing. While Courteney seemed to be wearing just her engagement ring, previous reports have suggested that she and Johnny would be marrying soon. Earlier this summer it was said that Ed Sheeran, who introduced the pair, would act as best man at the wedding. "Not only is Ed great mates with Johnny, he is a close friend of Courteney's," an insider told . "Ed said yes immediately when asked. Johnny's other best pal, singer Foy Vance, is also an usher. It's between him and Ed for best man – they'll probably share it."

    Johnny McDaid flashed a new ring on his wedding finger on Sunday

    Courteney and Johnny were believed to be holding two ceremonies – one stateside for the actress' friends and family, and one in the singer's native Northern Ireland. Less than a month after revealing their engagement, the couple holidayed to the Emerald Isle where they celebrated Johnny's 38th birthday and were also thought to be scouting for wedding venues. "We all love her," said Johnny's mother Pauline, who added that Courteney "felt at home" during her stay. "John is very much a Derryman. So to be able to show Courteney around where he grew up and around his history, he was as proud as punch." The  beauty was previously married to David Arquette for 14 years, but the pair divorced last year. They are parents to ten-year-old daughter Coco.
