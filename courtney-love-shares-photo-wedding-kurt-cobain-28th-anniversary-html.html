Courtney Love shares a photo from her wedding to Kurt Cobain on what would be their 28th anniversary

Courtney Love and Kurt Cobain were the rock star couple of the 90s, and their relationship captured the public's attention. 

Just days after honoring the late Nirvana front man,Love again took to Instagram on Monday in honor of their wedding 28 years ago.

The 55-year-old singer shared a photo from their 1992 wedding in Honolulu Hawai'i and reminisced on the decades since. 

Decades ago: Courtney Love shared a photo from her 1992 wedding to Kurt Cobain, on what would have been their 28th wedding anniversary to Instagram on Monday

'28 years ago,' Love wrote. 'I recall feeling, deeply, delighted, dizzy, so in love, and knowing how lucky I was.' 

She shared her love for the late singer, who died by suicide just two years after their wedding. At the time in 1992, Nirvana was at the peak of their fame and Love was a star on the rise with her band Hole.

In the throwback wedding photo, the couple pose arm in arm, with the ocean and a cloudy sky behind, both holding white and red bouquets.  

Cobain was in his plaid pajamas, as he note ably was feeling lazy on their big day and didn't want to put on a suit. 

While Love wore a satin and white lace dress that once belonged to actress Frances Farmer. 

On what would have been their 28th wedding anniversary Love said she spoke with her sponsor, lit candles, 'chanted, diamoku,' walked her dog and enjoyed sushi with a close friend.

An article around the time of Frances' birth suggested that Love used heroin while knowingly pregnant, though she said her words were taken out of context. 

The incident ultimately led to Frances being taken out of her parents care for a time beginning when she was two weeks old. 

They both attempted to get clean in March before welcoming their daughter, now 27, but quickly left their detox programs.  

After their wedding Cobain continued to struggle with drug use, depression and isolation. He was in rehab in early 1994, and left just days before his death in Seattle, on April 5.

Love and her daughter Frances posted photos of Cobain on Thursday and what would have been his 53rd birthday.  

Frances honored her father by sharing a touching photo of him holding her in his arms as a young baby as he stared deep into her eyes. While Love paid tribute by posting a picture of Cobain playing an acoustic guitar with a simple but sweet heart emoji in the caption. 

Love was married to the guitarist and singer from 1992 until his death, following a long struggle with depression and heroin addiction.

Frances Bean inherited 37% of her late father's estate in December 2009.

'It's almost like this big, giant loan that I'll never get rid of,' she revealed during an episode of the RuPaul: What's The Tee? podcast.

'I have an almost foreign relationship to it or guilt because it feels like money from somebody that I've never met, let alone earned myself.'
