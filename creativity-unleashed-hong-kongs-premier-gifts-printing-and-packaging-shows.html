Creativity unleashed: Hong Kong's premier gifts, printing and packaging shows

The vibrant Hong Kong Gifts & Premium Fair, the Hong Kong International Printing & Packaging Fair and the inaugural DeLuxe PrintPack Hong Kong will soon enliven the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre and AsiaWorld-Expo. 

Running concurrently from April 27-30, the premier industry events aim to promote cross-industry and cross-sector collaboration. They will showcase over 4,000 exhibitors from 21 countries and regions at the Gifts & Premium show and around 500 exhibitors at the two Printing & Packaging shows. 

Complementing the physical exhibitions, the online business matching service 'Click2Match' will facilitate networking from April 20 to May 7, enhancing connections and engagement across global markets. 

A platform for design excellence 

The Hall of Fine Designs will feature renowned global brands such as Hong Kong's Red A and Tiny Memory, as well as YASHICA from Japan, underscoring the fair's commitment to showcasing global design excellence.  

The Hong Kong Exporters' Association will host a pavilion dedicated to local talent, highlighting the region's strength in product design and branding. In addition, visitors will have the opportunity to explore the innovative entries of the Hong Kong Smart Design Awards, celebrating the creative achievements of local brands. 

 Dynamic zones and product showcases 

The show features a dedicated Cultural & Creative Corner with nearly 30 exhibitors. In particular, the China Gifts Zone features designs with local cultural features and products associated with intangible cultural heritage from exhibitors in Shandong and Henan provinces.  

Featured Cultural & Creative Corner exhibitors include:  

  • Deqing Xingyitai Import & Export Co. Ltd. (Booth 5E-D22) designs and markets a vibrant collection of bamboo hand fans in a variety of colours, patterns and materials. 
  • Changzhou Wisdom International Trading Co. (Booth 5E-F03), a manufacturer of products ranging from dinnerware sets to delicate teapots, including bone ceramic teacup collections.  

Meanwhile, the new Outdoor & Sporting Goods Zone will add to the excitement of the show, offering a glimpse of the latest trends and distinctive products, from high-fashion designer brands to the latest outdoor and sporting equipment. 

Enhancing industry knowledge through seminars 

At the heart of the show will be a series of seminars offering insights into consumer market trends, sustainable practices and opportunities within the silver economy. These sessions are designed to deepen the understanding of industry players and foster meaningful connections between them. 

Advances in printing and packaging 

The Hong Kong International Printing & Packaging Fair will feature eco-friendly innovations in the Green Printing & Packaging Solutions Zone, showcasing biodegradable materials and upcycled products. This focus is in line with global environmental concerns and new legislative requirements for packaging materials. 

The Food & Beverage Packaging Solutions Zone will address the needs of the food industry with specialised packaging for fresh, chilled and processed foods. In addition, the E-commerce Packaging Solutions Zone will showcase innovative solutions tailored to the rapidly growing online retail market. 

Exhibitors showcasing innovative solutions include: 

  • United Label-Trade Ltd (Booth 6-J08), showcasing temperature change labels that change colour according to temperature. 
  • Shenzhen HongBang Packaging Production Co., Ltd. (Booth 6-G23), showcasing a variety of packaging options including biodegradable and high temperature resistant bags. 
  • Sangil Eco and Paper Bag Co., Ltd. (Booth 3-F02), offering environmentally friendly paper bags made from a mix of virgin and recycled materials certified to the FSC MIX standard. 

 Luxury packaging show makes debut 

The debut of DeLuxe PrintPack will showcase premium packaging solutions for luxury goods such as jewellery, cosmetics and gourmet foods. This segment will enhance brand identity and consumer appeal through innovative packaging design. 

The shows will also feature forums and networking activities to discuss industry trends and hot topics such as sustainable packaging and luxury branding. These discussions aim to inspire innovative designs and strategies that promote environmentally friendly practices and enhance brand perception through luxury packaging. 

Featured DeLuxe PrintPack exhibitors include:  

  • RR Donnelley Asia Printing Solutions Limited (Booth 3-A08), which will showcase a Christmas Countdown Gift Box that ingeniously combines functionality and aesthetics. 
  • R&C Printing Products Solutions Limited (Booth 3-A10), offering a range of paper packaging solutions for products including wine glass, apparel and cosmetics. 
  • Wing Chun Gift Boxes Products (HK) Co. Limited (Booth 3-B01), featuring the XO Wine Gift Box with delicate craftsmanship. 

 Exploring trends through forums and networking  

The shows will further feature forums and networking activities to discuss industry trends and hot topics. For example, the 'Less is More: Packaging for the Planet' seminar will explore how innovative design can produce eco-friendly packaging and strategies that companies can implement to encourage consumers to go green.  

The 'Unboxing Luxury: Explore the Impact of Deluxe Packaging on Brand Perception' seminar will explain how infusing 'soul' and 'personality' into packaging design can enhance brand equity. 

Award-winning entries from the Hong Kong Print Awards, the Hong Kong Digital Printing Award and ‘GAAHK x PolyU Design - Packaging Design’ will be on display, highlighting the continued innovation within the print packaging industry. 

 Be at the forefront of industry innovation 

Gift, print and packaging professionals are invited to register for these premier events to network with global market leaders, discover new products and gain key industry insights. 

With the events fast approaching, taking advantage of this opportunity can significantly benefit attendees looking to enhance their business prospects, forge new partnerships and stay ahead of the curve in their field.
