Curtain Bangs Short Hair: 20 Chic Ideas To Try In 2023

Curtain bangs are a simple addition to your hair that gives a touch of feminity to your already flawless personality. For sure it is versatile and can be styled in multiple ways to suit your appearance and preferences.

In this article, we will explore 20 chic ideas for curtain bangs on short hair for a sleek and polished look for a more effortless and tousled vibe. So without further ado, let's dive in and discover the perfect curtain bangs inspiration for you!

1. Lob With Curtain Bangs

A simple lob haircut is amazing on its own but if you add a beautiful fringe or curtain bangs, you will have an instant makeover. Talk to your hairstylist and try this hairstyle this year. To achieve this look, use your curling wand or straighting rod to create the wavy texture and add some of your favourite smoothing products.

Just make sure  your hair care products has some hold to it and voila you will be instant ready!

2. Short Bob with Curtain Bangs

Be it textured, straight, wavy, or crispy wispy hair, bob hair cut looks amazing no matter what. Like mentioned above, to achieve salon like finish, you need some nice haircare products to give you some volume and shine which has some hold to it.

All you need to do is add the products to your hair and give some waves to them using your rollers and finish it off with a spray for longetivity. 

3. Wavy Hair With Curtain Bangs

Bangs enhances the beauty of naturally wavy hair in several ways. Typically, a curtain bang is a face framing layers that creates a soft and flattering effect. When you do that, the waves and bangs helps accentuate your natural texture while drawing attention to your unique facial features.

Not only does this adds dimension to your hair but it also give your hair a carefree and bohemian vibe.

4. Bob Haircut With Bangs

This bob is cut to a sleek and even length, falling just above the shoulders, exactly on your neck. This look is great on its own but a little addition of bangs frames the face beautifully adding a touch of sophistication to any look.

This haircut is easy and the middle part adds a modern touch that can be worn for both casual and formal occasions.

5. Beach Waves With Bangs

Intensify your beachy waves with a trendy addition - curtain bangs! Adding bangs to your shaggy lob hair will beautifully frame your face, giving it a fresh and youthful look. Whether you opt for full, blunt bangs or wispy, textured ones, they will perfectly complement your tousled waves.

Plus it creates a stylish and effortless vibe that can be tailored to suit your face shape and personal style.

6. Long Lob Hair

Layered texured lob looks good regardless of the hair color. But if you are someone with a natural dark hair or loves to dye your hair dark, then this haircut will serve the best look. You can leave the hairstyle as it is or add waves to enhance your natural exure.

Not only this hairstyle will enhance your facial feature, but will also bring out your eyes and makes your face look brigh and flawless.

7. Short Shag

To maintain a short shag haircut with curtain bangs at home, there are a few steps to follow religiously. It a long list of products but trust your stylist when they recommend products during your sessions.

8. Modern Short Hair

The modern bob haircut on naturally dark or dyed hair showcases an unparalleled awesomeness. It is sleek, sophisticated and emphasizes on bringing out the natural shine and depth of your dark hair.

Not only doing that creates a contrast but it also leaves a clean line offering endless styling possibilities. 

9. Short Wavy Hair With Curtain Bangs

Undoubedtedly this hairstyle looks like an ultimate model-off-duty look. This short hairstyle emphasizes on drawing attention to your eyes while the bangs subtly sweeps over parts of the eyebrows.

To maintain this hairdo start by washing your hair with a gentle shampoo and conditioner. Then apply a lightweight styling product for additional texture. Then, use a diffuser attachment on your hairdryer to gently dry your hair, scrunching it upwards to encourage wave formation. Once dry, finish off by spraying a texturizing spray for added hold and definition.

10. Blonde Bob With Bangs

As we all know a curtain bang is a specific type of fringe that is parted in the middle and frames the face, creating a soft and stylish appearance. This addition of bangs gives a fashionable and modern choice for those wanting a chic and versatile look.

If you are not a big fan of blonde, you can opt out for colorful highlight, balayage, or any dark colors of your choice.

11. Short Shaggy Bob With Bangs

Give your hair a desired makeover this year by adding shaggy layers and curtain bangs for a start. Adding shaggy layers to your hair  crates an effortless look which is further amplified by adding curtain bangs.

With these additions, your hair looks fresh and carefree. So, what are you waiting for, step into the new year with a confident and fashionable hairstyle that will turn heads wherever you go.

12. Short Hair With Wispy Bangs

Anyone adoring short hair would love to add whispy bangs to their short hair for a fresh and stylish look. As for the color, you can choose a vibrant shade like a fiery red or a cool icy blue to make a bold statement.

Alternatively, you can leave the hair its natural color and let the whispy bangs enhance the overall look with their chic and playful vibe. You can choose to wear them straight and parted down the middle, or you can scrunch your hair to enhance the waves to create a classic curtain effect.

13. Pixie Cut with Curtain Bangs

The combination of curtain bangs and naturally straight hair gives an edgy aesthetic. As shown in the picture, the model has dark hair with crispy-sharp bangs which can be achieved by using hair wax and straightner.

You can leave the hair as it is or add some waves for effortless texture, while the curtain bangs help to soften the face and create a more feminine appearance.

14. Long Curtain Bangs For Short Hair

Give a touch of feminity and softness to your overall appearance by adding curtain bangs to a pixie cut so that it can be styled in various ways. As you can see in the picture, the bangs are longer than the other portion of your hair, you can sweep to the side or part down the middle

Given their length, these bangs can easily blend with the rest of the hair, allowing for seamless styling options.

15. Shoulder Length Hair With Curtain Bangs

Curtain bangs can also sits well with glossy straight hair as it is effortless and easy to maintain at home. To achieve this look by precision, use a round brush and top your hair off with a texturizing spray and then flick your straightener upwards and then inwards as you reach the ends of your length to create a C-curl.

Doing this step adds volume to your bangs making this cool cut desirable for any hair type.

16. Short Bob With Fringe Bangs

A dreamy blend of fringe and side swept bangs sits well with your short hair. This hairstyle is simple and gives you the freedom to rock it as it is without having to commit to your short hair. In addition to being dreamy, styling this look is easy too, all you have to do is blow-dry the bangs with a round brush, directing them to the side.

Then, add texture to the rest of the hair with the help of a flat iron or curling iron. Finally, use some setting spray and you are done. And yes, add dry shampoo in between washes to keep the hair looking fresh!

17. Colorful Short Hair

Buoy up your confidence by wearing your hair with colorful statement pixie cut with side swept bangs. Experiment with vibrant hues like electric blue, fiery red, yellow or pastel pink for a dramatic makeover.

These playful colors will bring a fresh dimension to your hairstyle, allowing you to express your unique personality. Don't be afraid to stand out on the crowd, just embrace the fun side of hair color and let your pixie cut shine!

18. French Bob With Curtain Bangs

Boost your confidence by wearing this timeless hairstyle and be prepared for some compliments and head turns. Incorporating bangs to your French bob will frame your face and give you a sophisticated look.

With a touch of poise, carry this trendy combination and embrace this stylish fusion with grace.

19. Wolf Cut With Curtain Bangs

Wolf cut is a low-maintenance hairstyle that can be achieved effortlessly in a few minutes. To get a salon like finish, start by applying styling product to define the ends and add volume while tousling your hair with your fingers to create a messy texture. 

Then, sweep the curtain bangs to the side for a soft and face-framing effect. This hairstyle is quick and stylish making it perfect for any day!

20. Middle Part Bob

To achieve a polished look in under 5 minutes, there are simple steps to follow. Start by parting your hair down the middle and comb it thoroughly to detangle any knots. Then, straighten your hair using a flat iron.

Slowly, use a round brush to blow-dry your curtain bangs way from your face, giving them a curtain effect. Lastly, finish the look by applying a small amount of hair serum to add shine and tame any flyaways.
