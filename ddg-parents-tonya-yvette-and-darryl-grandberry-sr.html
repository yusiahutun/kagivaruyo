Who Are DDG Parents?

DDG AKA PontiacMadeDDG, an American YouTuber who popularly creates parody, skit, and prank videos, is the son of Tonya Yvette and Darryl Grandberry Sr. His parents has been described as social media stars. The rapper has turned his vlogging venture into a family business over the years. His brother DUB and nephew Woo Wop frequently appear in his videos.

His parents welcomed a son named DDG on October 10, 1997, in Pontiac, Michigan. While his father works in the automotive business, his mother is a retail employee. He had a challenging upbringing because his family resided in an area plagued by crime and gang violence. Tiara is the name of his younger sister. He also lost a brother in a shooting in 2014, and this tragedy had a significant effect on the little boy’s character.

The graduate of the International Technology Academy was DDG. He also spent a brief time studying at Central Michigan University. In terms of his romantic relationships, the YouTuber is seeing Kennedy Cymone. The couple frequently works together on the videos for DDG.

On July 26, 2014, the ‘PontiacMadeDDG VLOGS’ channel for PontiacMadeDDG was launched. It includes the YouTuber’s videos about his day-to-day activities. I Passed Out On The Roller Coaster, Naked Beach Day, Buying My Dream Car At 19, and Reacting To My Girlfriend’s Smash Or Pass Video are some of the most-seen videos on this channel.

This channel has received more than 1.3 million subscribers and around 177 million views as of March 2018. Gaming videos may be found on PontiacMadeDDG’s third channel, “GamingWithDDG,” and on his fourth channel, “The DDG Family,” which also includes intriguing footage.

PontiacMadeDDG is a vocalist in addition to a YouTuber. In 2015, he posted his debut song “Dope” to SoundCloud. The songs “G.O.A.T” and “Big Boat” came next. The tracks “Lettuce,” “Givenchy,” and “Take Me Serious” are some of his most recent.

DDG’s mother is called Tonya Yvette. Similar to her son, Tonya Yvette Grandberry, commonly referred to as MAMA DDG, has a sizable online following. Tonya informs the rapper that he was adopted in another song. That demonstrates how close an apple falls to the tree. Tonya adores DDG’s girlfriend in addition to her wonderful bond with her son. The three smiled broadly as they posed for a picture during the rapper’s 25th birthday celebration.

“Happy birthday son,” Tonya wrote in an Instagram caption. “I love you with everything in me. And I’m so proud of you.”
