Genesis Inamagua, 5, is found safely hours after she was last seen in New York City supermarket hold

A missing 5-year-old girl was found safe after she was last seen in a New York City supermarket holding the hand of a 75-year-old stranger- an adult who was arrested and now faces charges. 

Genesis Inamagua was last seen at around 9am Tuesday in a Queens supermarket and was reported missing about four hours later, according to police.

'The missing has been found safe,' the NYPD tweeted at about 4:40 pm, nearly an hour after officials posted a missing persons report. 

A surveillance photo shows the girl accompanied by an older man wearing a blue shirt and khaki pants at Foodtown Supermarket at 76-10 37 Avenue in Queens.    

The young girl was holding the hand of the 75-year-old man who the mother recognized from the neighborhood but did not know personally, sources told CBS
