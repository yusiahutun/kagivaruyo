The best gold bridesmaid dresses 2022

Galvan

Galvan

Credit: MYTHERESA

A true gold design, this halterneck dress from Galvan has a beautifully simple silhouette with a fluted hemline and a slight drape. At the back it fastens with a party-ready satin bow.

Michelle Mason

Michelle Mason

Credit: Farfetch

This Michelle Mason wrap gown is an excellent choice for a more traditional wedding. Its colour is elegant and subtle and due to its wrap fastening, it's a design that will look great on multiple body shapes.

Marchesa Notte Bridesmaids

Marchesa Notte Bridesmaids

Credit: Farfetch

This Marchesa Notte design is certainly sparkly but it's not OTT. Despite it being embellished head to toe, the silhouette remains simple which balances it out perfectly.

Monique Lhuillier

Monique Lhuillier

Credit: Net-a-Porter

If you don't want matching bridesmaids but have picked one colour so a common thread runs through the bridal party, this is a beautiful one-off gold option. Romantic and structured, the sheer tulle features tiny gold polka dots all over.

Zimmermann

Zimmermann

Credit: MYTHERESA

Another great choice if you want your bridesmaids to be in one colour but look individual. This matte gold Zimmermann design is one that your bridesmaid will be able to wear over and over again post-wedding.

Asceno

Asceno

Credit: MYTHERESA

This light toffee Asceno silk slip dress is beautifully simple. Taking inspiration from Nineties' minimalism, it is cut on the bias for a fluid drape.
