Kate and Oliver Hudson's comments on their biological father Bill Hudson

While Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell are a legendary Hollywood couple, having been together for over four decades, two of the kids they share, Goldie's oldest Kate and Oliver Hudson, have developed just a strong a bond with their "Pa."

However, from time to time, the two actors have brought up their biological father, Bill Hudson, and given October 17 was in fact Bill's birthday, it surely brought up memories for the siblings.

The singer, one of the trio The Hudson Brothers, was married to Goldie from 1976 to 1980, after which he filed for divorce, and soon after left the family. He did remarry, tying the knot with Laverne & Shirley star Cindy Williams, who passed away earlier this year.

For a long time after Goldie and Bill's separation, the relationship between the dad and his children was strained. Since then, however, it has improved, with Oliver even acknowledging in 2018 that he had exchanged "some texts" with his estranged father.
