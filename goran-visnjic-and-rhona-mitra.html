About

Goran Visnjic is a 51 year old Croatian Actor. Born Goran Višnjić on 9th September, 1972 in Sibenik, Croatia, he is famous for Dr. Luka Kovac on E.R., Dr. John Woods on Extant in a career that spans 1988–present. His zodiac sign is Virgo.

Rhona Mitra is a 47 year old British Actress. Born Rhona Natasha Mitra on 9th August, 1976 in Paddington, London, England, UK, she is famous for her roles as Holly Marie Begins in the Fox teen and family drama series Party of Five, Geraldine in the 2000 action thriller film Get Carter, Kate Hedges in the 2002 comedy film Ali G Indahouse, Berlin in the 2003 crime drama-thriller film The Life of David Gale, the Bus Stop Bombshell in the 2003 comedy film Stuck on You, Tara Wilson in the multi-award-winning ABC legal comedy-drama series The Practice and its spin-off series Boston Legal, Detective Kit McGraw in the FX medical drama series Nip/Tuck, Major Eden Sinclair in the 2008 science fiction action film Doomsday, Sonja in the 2009 action horror film Underworld: Rise of the Lycans, Major Rachel Dalton in the Cinemax/Sky One original action-adventure/spy-drama series Strike Back, Dr. Rachel Scott in the TNT post-apocalyptic action-drama series The Last Ship, Erma in the 2018 Netflix action comedy film Game Over, Man!, and Mercy Graves in the CBS/CW superhero series Supergirl. Her zodiac sign is Leo.
