Hailey Bieber's Coachella manicure is perfect for festival season

The supermodel has shaped our summer beauty agenda with glow-in-the-dark nails...

    Hailey Bieber is quite literally unstoppable when it comes to influencing our beauty agenda’s. If Hailey has it, we want it.

    It was questionable whether she’d have the same stratospheric level of influence as she did in 2022, and it’s now abundantly clear she is going to continue her reign as the most powerful beauty icon on the entire planet.

    Recently, we were swooning over her natural looking pastel pink nails (also labelled 'milk nails' by TikTok’s beauty community), and her yellow ‘lemon meringue’ nails prior provided stunning spring inspo. For Coachella 2023, she went back to bright with the coolest festival nails ever.

    Some people go all out with their Coachella looks. Glitter, fringe and everything in between. But not Hailey. She kept her outfit minimal, and let her funky manicure steal the limelight (quite literally).

    At the helm of celebrity nail artist Zola Ganzorigt aka @nailsbyzola, the supermodel donned the most tasteful lime green nails we’ve ever seen, finished off with cured gold squiggles to give them a touch of signature Hailey glam. Her plain white cropped top with super baggy jeans provided the perfect canvas to let her major manicure shine.

    The best thing about Zola, is she shares her nail knowledge on Instagram for us to recreate. She uploaded a close up of the manicure saying: “After trying 50 different colors, we came up with the perfect shade for Coachella.  Always enjoy our scientific experiment”. Our kind of science...

    Fans were momentarily concerned for her husband Justin Bieber after a video emerged online of the singing sensation slouched over a barrier at the festival, with Hailey gently caressing his back. 

    The Rhode beauty founder was officially named 2022’s most popular beauty icon and was responsible for inspiring our effortless yet iridescent beauty aspirations in the form of dewy cheeks, glazed nails, her controversial brownie glazed lips, and the ‘clean girl’ aesthetic.

    Google searches for yellow nail polish were up 120% after she posted her delectable lemon meringue nails last month: cue skyrocketing searches for ‘glow in the dark’...
