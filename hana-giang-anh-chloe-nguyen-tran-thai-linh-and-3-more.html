Hana Giang Anh, Chloe Nguyen, Tran Thai Linh and 3 more of Vietnams top influencers what they

The land of pho, excellent coffee, floating markets, not to mention a crazy number of motorbikes and exceptionally warm people – there is never a dull moment when in Vietnam. Lo and behold, this is captured by the unique aesthetics of the country’s top influencers and there is really no reason why you shouldn’t follow them.

Tú Bình Thường @normaltus: 48,600 followers

Nothing is better than seeing a foodie get hyped up over local cuisine. This Hanoi foodie’s feed is going to make you want to take that next flight to Vietnam or deal with a grumbling stomach screaming for banh mi. Not only that, she also shares some of her recipes so keep an eye on her IGTV.

Meet the Nguyens, family of Tien Nguyen – Vietnam’s richest heiress

Hana Giang Anh @hanagianganh: 344,000 followers

Pilates, Zumba, Les Mills – name it, she can instruct you properly on how to do it. Hana is your all-around fitness guru, plus add the fact that she surfs, she paddle-boards, bikes, she plays the guitar and, most importantly, she is also a mum.

Tran Thai Linh @tranthailinh: 62,800 followers

An all-around male Instagram influencer, Tran Thai Linh’s feed offers a variety of stuff, from his fitness routines to his beauty regimen (yes, he has one), his travels within Vietnam and outside the country as well and his clothing options (or lack of clothes) are way too good to be true.

How Vietnam’s meaty banh mi went global – then became a vegan thrill

Chloe Nguyen @bychloenguyen: 318,000 followers

Chloe is like a modern-day Cinderella. Her Instagram is so full of beauty, make-up and fashion posts that you'd think this is what she does all day. The grand thing about her is that she does keep everything simple; her make-up tutorials are subtle, just putting enough highlight on her features, but the result is extraordinary.

My Vietnamese Mum @myvietnamesemum: 56,800 followers

Food is an important aspect in any country and with exceptional food in Vietnam, we are bound to have another foodie in the list. But not just any foodie. The story behind this account is quite interesting. A Vietnamese mum is sending her child photos of what she eats every day and her kid decided to make an Instagram account out of it.

Blackchick Saigon Vietnam @tattooist.blackchic: 78k followers

When they said that tattoo parlours were a dime a dozen in Vietnam, they weren't wrong. But some artists stand out simply because their aesthetic is hard to replicate. And that’s why Blackchick’s feed is amazing. From her minimalist black and white tattoo shop to her really clean artistic lines, she deserves to be called an artist.
