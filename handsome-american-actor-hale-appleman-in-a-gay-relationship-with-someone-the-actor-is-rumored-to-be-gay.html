Hale Appleman's Current Relationship Status; Does He Have a Wife?

American actor Hale Appleman started his career in the 2006 movie, Beautiful Ohio but he initially gained success from the 2007 controversial comedy movie, Teeth.  

The handsome actor from New York is one of the most charismatic actors of his generation. In the series, The Magician, he plays the role of bad boy, heartthrob, debaucherous, naughty, sophisticated, aesthetic dandy.

Now judging by their relationship it looks like the pair could be married. Then Stephanie herself thought that he was her husband. In the tweet she playfully stated,

'You and my husband look really sweet together but never forget he's mine."

So it's safe to say the actor might be and might not be gay as he looks really happy with Stephanie, regardless of the nature of their relationship. The actor has yet to come out in the open and speak the truth about his marriage.

On the other hand, Appleman's online behavior where he often updates suspicious pictures and videos of people of the same gender makes it harder to know about the truth of the actor's sexuality. Hopefully, Hale will clear the doubt to his fans himself soon.

Update: Hale Appleman's Relationship!

Hale Appleman appears to be single these days. The Magicians star who was thought to have been married to Stephanie Falcon Simbari turns out to be false. The lady is actually in a relationship with her boyfriend Tyler Corcoran. She is even pregnant with their child and soon to give birth.
