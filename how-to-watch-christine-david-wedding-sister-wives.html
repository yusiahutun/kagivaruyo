Christine Brown is one of the stars of Sister Wives, TLC’s reality TV series following the lives of a polygamist family consisting of husband Kody Brown, his four wives—Robyn, Meri, Janelle, and Christine—and their 18 children. The series premiered in 2010 and has since become one of TLC’s most popular shows. Christine was Kody’s third wife. They share six children: son Paedon, and daughters Aspyn, Mykelti, Gwendlyn, Ysabel, and Truely.
