Fans of hit US TV show 90210 will know that Jessica Stroup's character Silver has started dating an older man in the form of new boyfriend Greg.

And while things between the couple are still in the early stages in the episodes that are currently airing in America, it seems like Silver and Greg's relationship will soon be heading in a different direction.

Actress Jessica was spotted on the set of the programme in Beverly Hills yesterday sporting what appeared to be a baby bump, sparking speculation she is set to fall pregnant with Greg's baby.

Something to tell us? Jessica Stroup was seen on the set of 90210 yesterday sporting what appeared to be a baby bump

The 25-year-old actress was seen wearing a turquoise and blue striped dress with a purple top as she shot scenes alongside co-stars Jessica Lowndes and AnnaLynne McCord.

The finale of the third season of the show saw Silver decide to give the relationship with Greg a go even after he told her he was divorced and with two children.

And talking about the relationship recently, Jessica said: 'I think it's the quality that she was lacking the most from her previous relationships and she's looking for something new, even though kids and an ex-wife and all this stuff is a lot for this fresh out of high-schooler.'

Blooming: The growing belly was clear to see as the cast shot scenes on Huntington Beach

Talking about the impact Greg's children have on their relationship, Jessica added to E! News: 'They become a very, very vital role in what makes up Silver and Greg's relationship and she tries to do the best she can even though she hasn't really had a mother figure her whole life.'

While most of the attention was on Jessica's baby bump as the cast shot the scenes, she had some competition from Shenae Grimes and Jessica Lownde, who displayed their endless legs in sexy outfits for the shoot.

Shenae, 22, was seen strutting her stuff in some red killer heels and a flimsy mustard dress, which was cinched in at her tiny waist with a brown leather belt.

Killer heels: Shenae Grimes teamed a flimsy mustard dress with some sky high red shoes

Meanwhile 23-year-old Jessica teamed a turquoise ensemble with a contrasting blue belt and some brown wedges.

The brunettes looked stunning as they paraded their pins in between takes.

But it was their co-star AnnaLynne McCord who received the male attention on set.

Perfect pins: Jessica Lowndes wore a turquoise dress with a blue belt and some brown wedges

The 24-year-old had a surprise visit from her boyfriend of several months, Prison Break actor Dominic Purcell.

And the Australian, who is 17 years her senior, could barely keep his hands off his girlfriend as he kissed and caressed her when she took a break from filming.

The visit clearly boosted AnnaLynne’s spirits, as she spotted beaming from ear to ear once she got back to work after a quick smooch with her beau.

Surprise!: AnnaLynne McCord received a visit from boyfriend Dominic Purcell while filming 90210

A tender tryst: AnnaLynne enjoyed a romantic moment with her beau in between takes

Good mood: The surprise visit from her boyfriend clearly boosted AnnaLynne's spirits

AnnaLynne was wearing a more modest outfit than her co-stars, which consisted of a flowing floor-length black skirt and green jumper.

But the actress still managed to flash some flesh, as the cropped number showed a hint of her flat stomach.

AnnaLynne’s outfit was topped off by a large white necklace and a colourful headband.

Warming up: Shenae (pictured with co-star Tristan Wilds) kept cosy in a cardigan when she wasn't filming

The leading ladies of the drama series may have been sporting some skimpy outfits, but they appeared to be a little underdressed for the cool Californian weather.

In between scenes Shenae was seen covering up with a cosy multi-coloured cardigan to keep her warm.

The girls were joined by co-stars Jessica Stroup and Tristan Wilds as they filmed scenes at the beach location.
