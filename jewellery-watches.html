20 of the best ear cuffs, from sleek gold to sparkling diamonds

De Beers

Pros

De Beers’ Dewdrop collection is a delicate ode to the beauty of nature: the larger bezel-set stones represent the beads of dew that form on the petals of flowers. This wide ear cuff is crafted from 18kt white-gold and studded with diamonds totalling 0.24 carats. Wear it at the top of your ear as a solo statement.

Monica Vinader

Monica Vinader

The hammered finish of Monica Vinader's 18kt gold-vermeil ear cuff creates a rustic feel. Team it with the brand's matching huggie hoops for the full look.

Mejuri

Mejuri's slick two-tone ear cuff combines sterling silver with 24kt gold-vermeil: a thick layer of solid gold plating that won't tarnish over time.

Boucheron

Contrasting textures and tones highlight the exquisite craftsmanship in Boucheron’s cuff. The four bands create the illusion of four separate stacked hoops, while round-cut diamonds ensure a sparkling finish.

Charlotte Chesnais

Charlotte Chesnais

Smooth, rippling lines are a Charlotte Chesnais signature, and this yellow-gold ear cuff is a sleek way to invest. Slip it around the lower edge of your ear, just above the lobe.

The Alkemistry

The Alkemistry

Threaded with creamy white pearls, this gold-plated ear cuff is equal parts sweet and subversive.

Rows of lab-grown diamonds bring a precious feel to Alondra's ear cuff, which is vermeil-plated with 18kt yellow gold and features a flat back to ensure a close fit around the ear.

Jimmy Choo

Jimmy Choo

A dazzling choice for the most glamorous of nights, Jimmy Choo's sparkling cuff wraps around the top of your ear, leaving a trail of glinting stones to skim your shoulder. Wear it with side-swept hair to keep the drama to a maximum.

Repossi

From Repossi’s sculptural Serti sur Vide collection, this striking cuff features a pave-set band that supports a spectacular solitaire diamond, designed to nestle perfectly in your right ear.

Anissa Kermiche

Anissa Kermiche

From Anissa Kermiche's new, more accessible collection, this gold-plated ear cuff is punctuated with pearls and delicate cubic zirconias. Wear it as the Parisian designer would: with tousled hair swept over the opposite shoulder.

Missoma

Missoma has long been one of our favourite affordable jewellery brands: it's the place to often turn to for gifts (as well as treats for ourselves).

This gold-vermeil ear cuff is designed to look like three seamlessly stacked separate pieces, with a row of cubic-zirconia studs sparkling at the centre.

Otiumberg

Otiumberg is another demi-fine favourite that uses recycled raw materials and sustainable production in its popular pieces.

Made in collaboration with Australian fashion brand Camilla and Marc, this cuff is formed from repurposed 18kt gold vermeil and sculpted to appear as two separate hoops: one of which is studded with white topaz stones. Wear it alone or stack it alongside the brand’s other designs for maximum impact.

Shay

Shay's intricate two-tiered ear cuff is delicate yet memorable, with a total of 0.58 carats in sparkling white diamonds set along the curved gold strands.

Kimaï

Disrupting the traditional world of fine jewellery, Kimaï works exclusively with lab-grown diamonds, which the design duo set into 18kt recycled gold to create new pieces from old materials. The results, however, are thoroughly modern: this ear cuff is shaped like a love-heart and set with pavé that will glimmer under lights.

Tiffany & Co

Tiffany & Co
