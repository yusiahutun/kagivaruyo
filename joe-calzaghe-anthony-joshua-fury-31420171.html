Joe Calzaghes desperate plea to Anthony Joshua and Tyson Fury to save boxing

Joe Calzaghe has told Anthony Joshua and Tyson Fury they need to get in the ring to save boxing from the YouTubers.

Calzaghe - Britain’s most successful world champion - reckons only by regular big fights and fewer champions can the screenagers be diverted from the novice celeb sluggers like Jake and Logan Paul.

“You have young fans who are not boxing fans, they are Jake Paul fans,” says Calzaghe, who ruled at super-middleweight for a decade, unbeaten, until he retired in 2009. “They tune in to watch him.
