Kesha is searching for a 'sugar daddy' after being dumped for the first time

Popstar Kesha revealed she was recently dumped for the first time in her life, and is now, on the hunt for a sugar daddy.

The music star is now 36-years-old, so to go over three decades without getting dumped is good going in our opinions. Here’s how she hopes to ‘mend her broken heart.’

Kesha on the hunt for a ‘sugar daddy’

The Tik Tok star recently featured on a Cosmopolitan video “roasting your dating profiles”, although said she had “no idea” why anyone was asking her as she just got dumped for the first time in her life.

“Can you f****** believe it? We’re all f*****, we’re all f******,” she exclaimed as the producers could be heard in fits of laughter behind the cameras.

In fact, she was just making a profile on the way to the shoot. Speaking on what she was searching for on her next venture, the popstar said she “mostly wants a sugar daddy.”

“I’ve just never had one, it sounds really fun,” she said. “I know it’s like anti-feminist of me but I want one, so DM me.”

Although the Animal star may want a sugar daddy, it seems like she’s staying away from celebrity dating app Raya for the foreseeable, as “everybody” is using the app.

However, she did reveal that she’s on “all the others,” so anyone looking for their chance with recently single Kesha, get swiping!

Kesha was ‘secretly engaged’ to ex-boyfriend

Although it’s not clear who the star’s talking about in her recent interview, she did infact reveal last year that she was “secretly engaged” to her ex-boyfriend who she remains “friends” with. However, she did not reveal his identity.

In an interview with Rolling Stone, the 36-year-old revealed that she wrote the song Too Far Gone after ending the engagement.

She was also formerly in linked to Brad Ashenfelter since 2014, and although have never commented on the break up, were last seen together in 2021.

The Grammy nominee has always kept her private life out of the public eye, although, herself and the movie producer had been spotted kissing, and togtether many items.

In June of this year, the star opened up about a health scare, sparked by freezing her eggs in a cover story with SELF.

However, the star revealed that she didn’t initally want to discuss her struggles, as she wanted the relationship to be kept private.

“I didn’t really want to mention the relationship, because I think making a family is everyone’s choice, and family can mean so many different things to everyone,” she explained.
