Holly Sonders is a Fox Sports reporter and host who has also played golf. She has a significant following on Instagram and often shares pictures from her modeling assignments. The rumored former couple reportedly met while Sonders was a sideline reporter for certain Big 12 Games.

Kliff Kingsbury’s Coaching Career

After Kliff Kingsbury retired from playing, he transitioned into football coaching. He began his coaching journey at the University of Houston, initially as part of the coaching staff. He gained significant recognition for his work with Houston’s offense, particularly with quarterback Case Keenum leading the team. As a result, he was eventually promoted to offensive coordinator. Kingsbury served at Houston from 2008 to 2011.

In 2012, Kliff Kingsbury joined the Texas A&M Aggies as their offensive coordinator. During his time with Texas A&M, he achieved notable success and was honored as the 2012 Footballscoop.com Offensive Coordinator of the Year.

In 2013, Kingsbury took on the role of head coach for the Texas Tech Red Raiders. He signed a contract with Texas Tech that extended until 2018. However, once his contract expired, he left the university. On December 5, 2018, he joined the University of Southern California (USC) as their offensive coordinator.

Currently, Kliff Kingsbury is serving as the head coach for the Arizona Cardinals. He was signed by the Cardinals on January 8, 2019, with a four-year contract.
