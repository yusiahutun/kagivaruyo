Kourtney Kardashian and Travis Barker's relationship timeline

The eldest Kardashian sister is a doting mother…

    Kourtney Kardashian, 43, and her adoring beau, Travis Barker, 47, definitely hold the title of one of the most loved-up celebrity couples.

    Whether it's a major PDA moment on the red carpet or an intimate post on Instagram, it's safe to say the founder and the  star are not afraid to show just how smitten they are, and, since tying the knot last year, their relationship continues to go from strength to strength.

    The now-married couple went on to have three weddings in total which were well documented in a Hulu special, which aired on 13 April. The behind-the-scenes film saw the couple's nuptials captured in a series of their own footage. Amongst the biggest stand-outs from the episode was a touching moment from Kourtney's mom, Kris Jenner, who gave her eldest daughter her wedding ring from their late father, Rob Kardashian. 

    When did Kourtney and Travis meet?

    The pair first met in 2006 when they were part of the same social circle. Travis was dating  Paris Hilton at the time, who was friends with Kourtney's younger sister, Kim Kardashian. 

    Despite being in a relationship with the famous heiress, it was always Kourtney who the famous drummer held a candle for as he revealed on reality show, , that his affection for the brunette beauty led him to move to the same gated community in Calabasas in 2017, after first moving to the area in 2007.

    "I knew she lived in Calabasas, and I knew she was the love of my life, and I didn't even know her like that, but I just knew if I couldn't date her or be with her because she was with someone else that at least I could live by her," he revealed. Kourtney had been dating her ex Scott Disick until 2015. 

    After being friends for years, the duo first sparked dating rumors in 2018, after being spotted heading out for cozy vegan dinner together in LA and attending Hillsong’s evening church service.

    It wasn't until February 2018 that the pair went Instagram official after celebrating a loved-up Valentine's Day together. The happy couple both posted a photo holding hands in a car, showing off Kourtney's picture-perfect red Valentine's Day nails. So sweet!

    When did they get engaged?

    Travis popped the question on 16 October 2021 on the beach at the Rosewood Miramar in Santa Barbara, California. The touching moment saw the musician on one knee in the middle of a huge love heart made out of red roses.

    When did they get married?

    The stars documented their lavish three-ceremony nuptials in a Hulu special despite Kourtney previously revealing that walking down the aisle wasn't something she'd ever been desperate to do. The pair certainly didn't hold back with their celebrations which ended with an stunning  Italian wedding on 22 May 2022.

     The bride and groom opted for an extravagant four-day celebration in Portofino, hosted and dressed by During the episode, Kourtney revealed that she had only ever stayed with the fashion moguls when visiting the idyllic location. "They so generously welcome us and the kids and so Domenico [Dolce] and G had said Domenico would love to design your dress and throw you your wedding," the star revealed.

    The guest list included the likes of celebrity pals, Megan Fox and Machine Gun Kelly,  Mark Hoppus and Skye Everly,  Simon Huck and Phil Riportella and Stephanie Shepherd.

    The couple - who legally married in Santa Barbara before heading overseas - exchanged vows in front of their close friends and family during a romantic ceremony at the fashion house's L'Ulivetta villa. They then enjoyed a stunning reception at the medieval castle Castello Brown. Their Santa Barbara wedding was a low-key affair and saw the newlyweds joined by Kourtney's grandmother and Travis' dad, who were unable to make it overseas to Portofino.

    The husband-and-wife duo was certainly prepared to say 'I Do', as they also had an unofficial wedding in Las Vegas on 4 April 2022 after the 64th Annual Grammy Awards, complete with their own 'Elvis Presely' officiant.

    Do they have children?

    Whilst the pair have been documenting their ongoing IVF journey, it's safe to say they couldn't be more besotted parents and step-parents to their children.

    The wellness mogul shares her daughter Penelope, ten, and sons Mason, 13,  and Reign, eight, with ex Scott Disick.

    Meanwhile, Travis is an adoring father to his step-daughter Atiana, 24, son Landon, 19 and daughter Alabama, 17, whom he shares with his ex-wife Shanna Moakler. The former couple divorced in 2008.
