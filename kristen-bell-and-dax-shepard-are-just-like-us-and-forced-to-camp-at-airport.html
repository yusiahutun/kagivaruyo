Kristen Bell and Dax Shepard are 'just like us' and forced to camp at airport

Kristen Bell, her husband Dax Shepard, and their two children were forced to camp at the airport after a nine-hour delay with their flight. The pair were reportedly “kicked out” of the airport after spending $600 dollars on blankets and pillows.

The Hollywood couple had quite the adventure on Wednesday evening (July 26, 2023) and documented their hilarious experience at the airport in a new Instagram video. Kristen and Dax set up a “sleepover” area at Boston’s Logan International Airport after their flight got delayed for nine hours.

Kristen Bell and Dax Shepard stranded at the airport

Kristen, Dax, and their two daughters, Lincoln, 10, and Delta, 8, were stranded at the airport due to flight delays but they managed to find the good in a bad situation.

The pair shared fun videos, documenting their time at their airport, which included setting up a sleepover area, buying some essentials, and sharing interesting details about their whole experience.

“Stranded at the Boston airport, 9 hours of delays,” The Good Place actress wrote on Instagram.

Dax shared an Instagram reel from their “sleepover” setup and revealed that they spent a total of $600 on blankets and neck pillows. Kristen even called their “bedroom” perfect.

Kristen and Dax were reportedly “kicked out”

In the same Instagram post, Dax revealed that they were forced to camp at the airport because they couldn’t find any hotel rooms in close proximity.

Kristen shared several posts on her Instagram Stories, saying that they made “quite the home” there, and unveiled snaps of playing card games like Spades and Uno.

The celebrity couple faced further challenges when they were reportedly “kicked out” of the airport. Luckily, they managed to find a place to stay for the night.

“Thankfully we found friends of friends who offered us their attics and accepted us at 1 am!!!!!!” Kristen wrote in a follow-up post.

Fans call the couple “just like us”

Kristen and Dax’s Instagram Stories proved that they are just two normal people who could experience normal things like flight delays.

One person reacted: “I love that you guys are just normal people.”

Someone else joked: ” I truly don’t mean this to be mean but I didn’t think this stuff happened to rich people.”

A third fan added: “The family that sleeps on the airport floor together, stays together. That’s my armchair expert opinion.”
