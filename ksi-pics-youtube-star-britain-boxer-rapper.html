YouTubes Biggest Star In Britain Hollywood Life

KSI (born Olajide Olayinka Williams “JJ” Olatunji) is an English YouTuber and rapper. He built a following on the Internet by posting clips of him playing and commenting on FIFA’s soccer video games. 

Today, the “knowledge, strength, and integrity” internet personality has over 21 million subscribers and is on the list of the “130 most subscribed to channels” of all time on YouTube.

The hot shot then went onto gain even more popularity when he was infamously trolled by none other than fellow YouTuber Logan Paul. 

That’s right, we know you all remember when the two Internet sensations went head to head in the ring that one time!

As just one of his many super duper crazy Internet schemes, the American YouTuber decided he wanted to challenge the British YouTuber (known as KSIOlajideBT on the video-streaming service) to an official duel in Los Angeles. The boxing match was held at the Staples Center and lasted an intense six rounds.
