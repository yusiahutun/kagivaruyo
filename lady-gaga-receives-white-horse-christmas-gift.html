You'll never guess what Lady Gaga has been given for Christmas

    What do you buy the pop princess who has everything? A white horse! Lady Gaga received the steed as an early Christmas gift from her record company on Monday, and couldn't wait to show off the "White Angel" to her fans on Instagram.

    The singer shared a photo of the beautiful white horse on social media, which showed it wearing pink reigns with a bow and letter from her record company tied loosely around its neck.

"A heartfelt thank you to John Janick and Steve Berman, the whole Interscope family. I will ride, and care for, and love her forever. I was so surprised!"

    She later shared another photo of the horse eating in a stable, writing: "Night my sweet girl."
