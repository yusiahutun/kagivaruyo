Biography

Leon Russom was born in Little Rock, AR on December 6, 1941. After playing the role of Willis Frame on NBC’s Another World, Russom appeared in episodes of such popular television series as Mission: Impossible, Star Trek: Deep Space Nine, Prison Break, and The X-Files. He and 3rd Rock from the Sun actor French Stewart were both members of a Los Angeles-based theater company called Sacred Fools. Early in his career, he acted in two CBS soap operas: Love is a Many Splendored Thing and Guiding Light.
