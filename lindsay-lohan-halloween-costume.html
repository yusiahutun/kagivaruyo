She's a Cat, Duh! Check Out Lindsay Lohan's Sexy Halloween Costume

Lindsay Lohan turned up the sex appeal in a leather and lace, flesh-flashing feline costume at a London Halloween Party last night.

The actress' fierce ensemble is a far cry from her "Mean Girls" days. Instead of rocking Cady's freaky "ex-wife" costume, the celebrity decided to turn heads and bare it all with an evil kitten look.

Lohan styled her see-through bodysuit with matching black underwear, leather accessories, and sky-high, over-the-knee boots. Getting into the Halloween spirit, Lohan matched her reveling outfit with a black cat mask and topped off her look with fake blood dripping from her mouth.

At the venue, Lohan was pictured partying the night away and mingling with other guests.

Lohan obviously loves celebrating Halloween and dressing up, and over the years, she's had some great costumes.
