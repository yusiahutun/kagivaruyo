Lionel Messi tops Cristiano Ronaldo as FIFA 20 ratings are unveiled

Lionel Messi and Cristiano Ronaldo are undoubtedly still the best footballers in the world.

Barcelona forward Messi was the top goalscorer across Europe last term, while Ronaldo enjoyed a fairly strong start to life at Juventus.

It could be argued that the Argentine has had a better last 12 months than his Portuguese counterpart, scoring 51 goals for his club, compared to Ronaldo’s 28.

And FIFA 20 has rewarded Messi by giving him a higher rating than Ronaldo - even if it is only the skin of his teeth.


For the upcoming instalment, Messi has been given a 94 rating - the highest rating for current players on the whole game.



Ronaldo trails him closely on 93, with PSG trickster Neymar one behind the Portugal skipper on 92.

Belgian duo Eden Hazard and Kevin de Bruyne are joined by Atletico Madrid goalkeeper Jan Oblak on 91.

With a 90 rating and rounding off the top 10 are Liverpool pair Mohamed Salah and Virgil van Dijk, as well as Luka Modric and Marc-Andre ter Stegen.

EA Sports have revealed the full list of the top 100 players for FIFA 20.

And just missing a spot in the top 10 are the likes of Chelsea’s N’Golo Kante and Tottenham striker Harry Kane - both rated 89.

Towards the bottom of the pile, there is room for Manchester United cast off Romelu Lukaku following his switch to Inter Milan, as well as Manchester City new boy Rodri, with both players rated 85.


Top 10 rated players for FIFA 20:

Lionel Messi, FC Barcelona 94

Cristiano Ronaldo, Piemonte Calcio 93

Neymar Jr., PSG 92

Eden Hazard, Real Madrid 91

Kevin De Bruyne, Manchester City 91

Jan Oblak, Atlético de Madrid 91

Virgil van Dijk, Liverpool 90

Mohamed Salah, Liverpool 90

Luka Modric, Real Madrid 90

Marc-André ter Stegen, FC Barcelona 90
