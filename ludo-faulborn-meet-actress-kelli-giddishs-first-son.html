Who Is Kelli Giddish’s Son, Ludo Faulborn?

Ludo Faulborn is the first child of one of America’s most talented actresses, Kelli Giddish, and her then-husband, Lawrence Faulborn. He rose to fame as the son of the “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit” actress.

However, despite being a celebrity child, his parents shield him from the media. Consequently, details about Ludo’s upbringing are unknown. In addition, there are no details regarding his educational background and achievements. 

What Is Ludo Faulborn’s Age?

Actress Kelli Giddish’s son, Ludo Faulborn, was born on October 6, 2015. Hence, he is seven years old. Despite his age, Ludo has garnered the attention of his mother’s fans, who are curious about his upbringing.

However, due to his parent’s stringently private lifestyle, the adorable celebrity kid leads a somewhat normal lifestyle away from the prying eyes of the media and the glitz and glamor of fame.

Who Is Ludo Faulborn’s Mother?

Ludo’s mother, Kelli Giddish, was born Kelli Marie Giddish in Cumming, Georgia, on April 13, 1980. She is an American television, stage, and film actress. Giddish rose to prominence for portraying Diana Henry in the soap opera “All My Children” from 2005 to 2007.

In addition, the stunning actress appeared in the thriller “Death in Love” (2008), which premiered at the Sundance Film Festival. She also played the role of Courtney in the web series “The Burg.” 

However, she is best known for portraying NYPD Detective Amanda Rollins in the NBC crime-drama television series “Law & Order: Special Victims Unit” (2011-2023). Aside from acting, Giddish has also made appearances in music videos. 

The stunning actress appeared in the music video for “Nadine” by Project Jenny, Project Jan in 2011. In addition, Giddish appeared in the music video for “All Love is Lost” by Body Count, Giddish’s SVU co-star Ice-T’s band, from their 2017 album Bloodlust with Max Cavalera from Soulfly and Cavalera Conspiracy as a special guest in May 2018.

Who Is the Father of Kelli Giddish’s First Son?

Ludo Faulborn’s father is Lawrence Faulborn. Due to their private lives, there are no details regarding how the former couple met and when they started dating. However, Kelli Giddish married Lawrence Faulborn on June 20, 2015, in an intimate beach ceremony in New Smyrna Beach, Florida. A few months later, the couple welcomed their first child, Ludo, in October 2015.

Unfortunately, their marriage ended abruptly three years later in 2018. Giddish quietly split from her husband, Lawrence, for unknown reasons. However, reports suggest the couple had been through a lot in their short marriage, with him falling ill when their firstborn was only six months old. 

After her first marriage crashed, the TV star remarried a few years later. Ludo Faulborn is a young kid in his formative years. Hence, it is unknown if he will go into acting like his mother. However, Ludo has had a few appearances alongside his mother on screen.
