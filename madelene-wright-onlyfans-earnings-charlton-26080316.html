Footballer Madelene Wright admits earning 500k on OnlyFans after Charlton sacking

Madelene Wright has opened up about her pain at being sacked by Charlton Women and subsequent earnings of more than half a million pounds using OnlyFans.

With another video showing her inhaling balloons at a party, Charlton launched an investigation which saw her let go from her role at the club.

Saying that she felt guilty and embarrassed at the time of the sacking, Wright has turned her life around to earn a fortune after building a huge following on Instagram.
