(Re)generative Wool V-Neck Sweater

Every closet needs a staple winter sweater that goes with every bottom — from jeans and trousers to skirts — and the (Re)generative Wool V-Neck Sweater is mine. Since receiving it a few weeks ago, you’d be hard-pressed to find me not wearing it. I was immediately drawn to the look, loving the relaxed fit and high V-neck, but keep pulling it back out because of how seriously comfortable it is. As a sensitive-skin girl, wool can be tricky (and by that I mean unbearably itchy), so I was shocked to find that when I slipped on this sweater, it didn’t just not itch, but was shockingly soft with a blanket-like feel.

“As I transition out of skinny jeans, I decided the easiest way to do so was to go [with these],” wrote one shopper who said the jeans “fit like a glove” and have a “perfect” length. According to another person, the style is “comfy and stretchy,” while another fan deemed them “the best fitting jeans.” “I have two Kick-Out Crop jeans and love them,” they said, adding that the cotton-blend material is “soft and not stiff.”
