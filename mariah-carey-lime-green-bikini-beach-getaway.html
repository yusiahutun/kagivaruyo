Mariah Carey Splashes in the Ocean in a Lime Green Bikini During a Beach Getaway

Mariah Carey is still celebrating her birthday in style.

Earlier this week, the superstar touched down at a tropical destination for her 54th birthday on March 27. Now, she is continuing to share snaps and videos from her holiday, including a few that capture her wearing a neon green bikini top underneath a black wetsuit with matching green lining.

The "Fantasy" singer first shared a selfie of herself and her 11-year-old twins, Monroe and Moroccan. In the sweet photo, the mom of two seems to pose with a wrapped birthday gift while Monroe and Moroccan smile widely next to her.

"Me and my favorite people in the world 💙💜 #ariesmonthcontinues," she captioned the picture on Instagram.

In another Instagram post, Carey clipped together videos from her beach getaway set to the tune of her single with Damian Marley, "Cruise Control." The montage shows her splashing around in blue waters with boyfriend Bryan Tanaka and lounging on an inflatable floating swan. She accessorized her lime green bathing suit with reflective black sunglasses, a butterfly ring, and gold hoop earrings.

"On a lazy river in the aqua water… now playing 'Cruise Control' 3x on repeat! haaa ! Love you Lambs!!!!🦋❤️🐑," she wrote in the caption.

Fans and celebrity friends alike lost it in the singer's comments section.

Latto praised Carey's vacation looks, writing, "Omgggg Mimi you look SO GOOD!!!!!!"

And Busta Rhymes simply left a string of fire emojis under the video, "🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥."

"Name a family with better curls?? I'll wait," one fan added.

"BRONZED GODDESS YOU LOOK AMAZING 😍😍😍," another wrote.

On her birthday, Carey posted a video of herself diving into the ocean from the top of a yacht while wearing a black wetsuit with hot pink paneling.

She captioned the post, "Celebratory splash at 3:27 on 3.27! 🦋🍾🎉🌊."
