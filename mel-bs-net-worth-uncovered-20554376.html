Mel Bs net worth uncovered ahead of Celebrity Juice debut

Mel B has finished up a successful Spice Girls tour, but that doesn’t mean we have seen the end of her.

Tonight she will replace Paddy McGuinness on Celebrity Juice as the new team captain.

Mel B said in a statement: “I am so happy that I’m going to be a part of the Celebrity Juice family and thrilled to be a captain.

“My mission is to give Keith a run for his money. Watch out. Things are going to get even more crazy.”

Here we take a look at her at Mel B’s net worth ahead of her debut on Celebrity Juice.

Mel B net worth:

Mel B has an estimated net worth of $3million (£2.5m), according to the Celebrity Net Worth website.

This is a far cry away from the £37.9m she reportedly pulled in from The Spice Girls' glory days.

The girl group sold a jaw-dropping 75m albums and were in the Guinness Book of Records for “Most Consecutive Number Ones for a New Act”.

Mel B also went on several world tours which the group, which grossed $500-800m in total between 1996 and 2001.
