Millyz Disses 'Goofy' Machine Gun Kelly, Defends Jack Harlow

Millyz has taken aim at Machine Gun Kelly in a new freestyle, and sent a message to MGK’s rap rival Jack Harlow in the process.

Appearing on DJ Cosmic Kev’s The Come Up Show Live on Tuesday (November 21), the Massachusetts rapper fired a series of scathing bars at MGK, who he called “goofy,” and extended an offer to Harlow, telling him he does “murder for hire” — lyrically speaking, of course.

AD LOADING...

Millyz’s comments refer to Machine Gun Kelly dissing Jack Harlow earlier this year.

“That goofy white rapper tried to block me out the industry/ Hated on my come-up, I remember that shit vividly/ You only dissed on Jack because you know I do ’em viciously,” he rapped over N.O.R.E.’s “Blood Money Pt. 3” beat.

AD LOADING...

He later spit: “Listen, this is light shit, I know you won’t respond back/ ‘Cause beyond that, you know we really on that/ Rap a blood sport, Mortal Kombat/ Come on Jack, I do murder for hire, just send a contract.”

Millyz also posted a clip of the freestyle on Instagram and wrote in the comments section: “Yo @jackharlow ima handle this for u gang.”

Machine Gun Kelly dissed Jack Harlow in a freestyle over JAY-Z and Eminem‘s “Renegade” beat in May, claiming he stole Drake‘s flow.

“Make sure there’s no confusions/ I’m a great white, I can eat these barracudas/ See who I am? You’re stupid, it’s nice to meet you/ I just put this hole in the ground for you like a soccer cleat shoe/ I see why they call you Jackman, you jacked man’s whole swag/ Give Drake his flow back, man, I eat rappers like Pac-Man,” MGK rapped.

AD LOADING...

Millyz’s disdain for Machine Gun Kelly runs deep as he previously called the Cleveland rapper “garbage” during an interview with VladTV last year.

“MGK is garbage,” he said. “He can’t rap, but he got a good rockstar image, but like I’m a real rapper. I really break down bars, syllables and words, so I look past image and flow.
