Moselle Elementary donates more than $1,800 to flamingos | Local News

When Moselle Elementary students found out that a flamingo had been stolen from the Hattiesburg Zoo, resulting in the death of that bird and her mate, they wanted to do something to help.

Faith Shows, Maddox McCraw, Kagen Welch and Mason Meyers look at the flamingo exhibit.

The students organized “Fund the Flamingos.” They sold pink rubber-band bracelets, and, in a few weeks, the school raised $1,812.

The Beta Club took a field trip to the zoo on Friday to present the check.

Weslyn Blackwell looks at the flamingos.

Southern Miss student Devin Nottis, 19, was charged with grand larceny for stealing “GiGi” the flamingo during a fraternity scavenger hunt last month.
