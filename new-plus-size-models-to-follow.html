12 Plus-Size Models to Watch

In the last several years, the fashion industry has made major changes with regard to size inclusivity. Granted, designers still don’t make enough clothing for plus-size people, and being plus-size in the fashion industry is still a challenge, but there is undeniably more size representation in fashion than ever before. This evolution has made space for more plus-size models to not only shoulder their way into the industry but also to actually build a career in it. In fact, we're rounding up our favorite plus-size models on the rise. Check them out below — you're going to have a whole new list of people to follow when you're done.

Jazzmine Carthon

  • Instagram: @jazzminecarthon
  • Agency: IPM Model Management
  • You may have seen her in: Project Runway — as one of the first plus-size contestants to win the model portion of the competition — Sports Illustrated, campaigns for Good American, Ashley Stewart, Rainbeau Curves, and Gwynnie Bee, and NYFW runways for Michael Costello, Esmara by Heidi Klum, Coco & Ilia, and Leanne Marshall.
