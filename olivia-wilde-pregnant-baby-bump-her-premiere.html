Olivia Wilde shows off baby bump in sleek blue dress

    Actress Olivia Wilde looked every inch the glamorous mum-to-be when she showed off her blossoming baby bump on Thursday night.

    The star wore a form-fitting cobalt blue dress which accentuated her growing tummy at the Her premiere in LA.

    Olivia, 29, complemented the dazzling embellishments on her dress with a pair of kitten heels with metallic toe detailIing. 

    The pregnant actress and her fiancé Jason Sudeikis announced they were expecting their first child in October.

    Olivia, who has not disclosed her due date, first met 38-year-old actor Jason on the season finale of back in 2011.

    The pair began dating six months later and got engaged in January of this year, according to . They are thought to be tying the knot in New York next spring.

    Earlier this year, Olivia made no secret of her hopes to some day have a family with Jason.

    She told "I'm open-minded about how many, but three, which I love, is like a little party. I am not trying to have kids now – there's no strict plan for anything in my life."

    About her future husband, who she calls "the great love of my life", she said: "What happens, happens. He's so good with kids ... I've never before experienced looking at someone and thinking, 'That's who I want to raise a child with.'"

    Both Olivia and Jason have been previously married and divorced. Former House star Olivia was married to Italian prince and documentary filmmaker Tao Ruspoli for eight years until 2011, while Jason was married to TV writer and producer Kay Cannon for six years. The two split in 2010.
