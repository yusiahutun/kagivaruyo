Celebrate 39th-annual Labor Day parade

published on March 29, 2024
The 39th-annual Rock Island Labor Day Parade is this Monday, Sept. 4, starting at 9:30 a.m. The parade begins at Washington Jr. High School on the corner of 33rd Street and 18th Avenue and ends at Rock Island High School. More than 120 entries have registered, with many based on this year’s theme of “Planting Pride – Growing Together.” You can watch marching bands, a charro horse riding group, local schools, musical groups, organizations, churches, businesses, the Oscar Meyer Wienermobile, Santa, and more.
