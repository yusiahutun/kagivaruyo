Poppy Delevingne is the first Jo Malone London Girl

Image courtesy of Jo Malone London

"Poppy is such a dedicated, vivacious character, with huge international appeal," says Jo Malone London's global general manager Jean-Guillaume Trottier.

And it's for this reason that the iconic British fragrance brand with serious cult credentials has chosen to team up with model, designer and London It girl Poppy Delevingne, to work on a series of projects and events over the coming year.

A long-time champion of the brand, Delevingne's enduring signature cologne is one of the house's best-selling fragrances, Red Roses, and she even chose to scent her wedding with the summery, citrus tones of Orange Blossom.

"Jo Malone London is woven into the fabric of my life and I couldn't be happier to be working with a brand I love so much," she says. We can't wait to hear more about this exciting new partnership.
