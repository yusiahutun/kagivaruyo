Post Malone Girlfriend, Whos He Dating Now?Jamie

Jamie (2020 - present)

Post Malone is currently dating a woman known by fans as Jamie. The two have been linked since 2020, but not much is known about their relationship since Posty likes to keep it private.

TMZ broke the news of his girlfriend’s pregnancy and his engagement. “I’m excited for this next chapter in my life, I’m the happiest I’ve ever been, and for since I could remember I was sad,” he told the gossip site. “Time to take care of my body and my family and friends, and spread as much love as we can every day.”

In fact, he surprised Howard Stern with the announcement that he had a baby. “[At] 2:30 in the afternoon, I went and I kissed my baby girl,” Post said to which the radio host replied, “What baby girl? Are you referring to your girlfriend or [do] you have a baby?” To which Post said, “No, that’s my daughter.”

He talked on the podcast Call Her Daddy about how he proposed to her. “I had lost a significant amount of money at the table, and we go upstairs and I’m, like, off my rocker hammered,” he recalled. “I was like, ‘Hey, you wanna marry me? I got a ring and all this stuff.’ And then she said no.”

However, she told him to ask her the day after “And then I did,” he said. “And I was sober and it was nice.” He also revealed that she doesn’t listen to his songs at first listen. “A lot of the songs I write for her I don’t even play for her, because I’m terribly shy,” he recalled.
