Rachel Brosnahan has fun in a flirty pink skirt in a lipstick pattern at Independent Spirit Awards

She's best known to audiences as a comically gifted 1950s New York City Housewife in The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel.

But Rachel Brosnahan looked altogether more modern at the 2020 Film Independent Spirit Awards on Saturday, donning a flirty pink wrap maxi skirt featuring a high thigh slit and a lipstick kiss pattern.

The House Of Cards actress, 29, topped off her look with a maroon leather crop top that showed off her shoulders, chest and bare midriff. 

Girls wanna have fun: Rachel Brosnahan had a little fun while making a fashion statement at the 2020 Film Independent Spirit Awards held at Santa Monica Beach on Saturday

Rachel's high-waisted skirt was playful and feminine, flaunting her toned legs and figure.

Her strappy heeled sandals were in a shade of dark red velvet.

Brosnahan's hair was blond and slightly tussled, ever so slightly brushing her shoulders.

She sported delicate eye makeup and a stark red lip, and other than a pair of lovely silver earrings and some rings, she was light on jewelry for the daytime awards event, held at Santa Monica Beach in Santa Monica, California. 

Here's looking at you: She donned a flirty pink wrap maxi skirt featuring a high thigh slit and a lipstick kiss pattern

Hippy and lippy: Rachel's high-waisted skirt was playful and feminine, flaunting her toned legs and figure

Original look: Her strappy heeled sandals were in a shade of dark red velvet

Outside the venue, Rachel posed for photos with The Act's Joey King, who also wore a pink look to the awards.

And inside the event, she had the pleasure of presenting the Best Male Lead Actor Spirit Award with Octavia Spencer.

Uncut Gems actor Adam Sandler won the trophy. 

Outside the venue: Rachel posed for photos with The Act's Joey King, who also wore a pink look to the awards

Brosnahan is a Golden Globe and Primetime Emmy winner for her leading role on Marvelous Mrs. Maisel.

She has two feature films coming out with year, one called Ironbark, and the other titled I'm Your Woman.

Ironbark is a period piece concerning a Cold War spy, and it premiered last month at Sundance with Rachel in tow.
