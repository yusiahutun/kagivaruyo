Rihanna responds to fat shaming comments

Getty Images

Rihanna has responded adeptly to a few online trolls who made unnecessary, derogatory remarks about her weight.

Last week, a sports site ran a story entitled 'Is Rihanna Going to Make Being Fat the Hot New Trend?', alluding to the star's alleged weight gain. While the singer's fans rushed to her defence, she herself - arguably one of the most perfect looking women on the planet - kept quiet.

However, last night Rihanna broke her silence, responding with a meme shared on Instagram. The image features rapper Gucci Mane looking a little more portly and then more muscular.

Rihanna captioned it with a crying emoji, a seemingly sarcastic addition to prove that she really doesn't care what an archaic, misogynistic sports site thinks.
