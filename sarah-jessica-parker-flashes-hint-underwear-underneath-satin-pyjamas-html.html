Sarah Jessica Parker flashes a hint of her underwear underneath satin pyjamas

Her Sex And The City character, Carrie Bradshaw is considered to be one of the most fashionable people on the TV. 

So it perhaps comes as little surprise that Sarah Jessica Parker was selected for lingerie brand Intimissimi's latest campaign. 

The 54-year-old looks incredible in the latest images, which show her flashing a hint of her underwear underneath satin pyjamas.

The campaign is said to empower women and calls upon Sarah Jessica's 'intelligence, charisma and personal femininity' as they celebrate the launch of the Triangle Bra collection. 

The brand say they aim to celebrate femininity with spontaneity and a light-hearted attitude that all women can ‘wear’ in their own way, as they go about their everyday life.

The campaign will also extend to a television commercial which shows SJP in a Manhattan apartment, digging through her closet and deciding what to wear.

But she recently got to film in the iconic setting of that show, when the actress was seen taping her HBO series Divorce in West Harlem, Manhattan. 

Season 3 of the HBO dramedy is set to air later this year. 

Parker revealed details about the axed third Sex and the City film in an interview with iNews. 

Discussing the filming schedule for the planned third instalment in the franchise, she said: 'We were in the process of boarding Sex And The City 3, right when I finished this movie, and I kept telling my producing partners on that film…

'They said, 'We're fighting for those 53 [shooting] days.' I said, 'We don't need 53 days! We don't need 48 days! We only need 35 days.'

When questioned on whether a third Sex and the City movie was happening after all? SJP responded: 'No, this was before the studio pulled the plug, because of what happened with Kim.'
