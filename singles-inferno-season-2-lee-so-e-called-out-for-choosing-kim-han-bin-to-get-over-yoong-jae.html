'Single's Inferno' Season 2: Lee So-e called out for choosing Kim Han-bin to get over Yoong-jae

SASEUNGBONG-DO, SOUTH KOREA: 'Single's Inferno' Season 2 star Lee So-e recently won one of the challenges and got the chance to choose a boy of her choice to go to paradise with. Everyone believed that So-e will choose Kim Se-jun. But, the opposite happened and So-e went for Kim Han-bin. This was a shocking and very random choice. 

Surprising everyone with an unexpected choice, So-e was excited to explore the paradise with Han-bin. The panelists knew that So-e's choice was very unexpected but it was surely a better choice. She already expressed herself too much to Jo Yoong-jae and now with Se-jun showing interest in her, it seems that she feared getting her heart broken. Therefore, she is exploring her options. So-e further explained to Han-bin why exactly she chose him. She claimed, "I did think I would have the most fun if I came with you."
