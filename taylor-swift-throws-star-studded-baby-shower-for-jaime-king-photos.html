Taylor Swift Throws Star-Studded Baby Shower for Jaime King

Taylor Swift is the best godmother ... EVER!

The "Bad Blood" singer threw a star-studded baby shower for her pregnant BFF Jaime King -- and the Instagram pics will definitely make you jealous that you didn't make it on the guest list.

The 25-year-old star brought together a slew of famous friends to celebrate the "Hart of Dixie" star's new addition, including Gigi Hadid, Jessica Alba, ï»¿Sarah Hyland, Hailee Steinfeld, the members of HAIM, Selma Blair, Nina Dobrev and Emma Roberts.

In true Taylor form, a photo booth with fun props was available to capture all the best memories from the soiree!

"Baby shower/major throwdown for @jaime_king feat @gigihadid @babyhaim," she captioned one set of photos, before posting a pic of "the whole team."

The mama-to-be shared a sweet message about the hostess, writing: "How can I put this into words... @taylorswift is not only going to be an exquisite Godmother but one of the best friends one could ask for."

"Thank you Tay for throwing me such an insanely beautiful baby shower. Less then 12 hours after performing for 100k strong. I love you SO MUCH xx @babyhaim @gigihadid," she continued, referring to Swift's sold-out concert the night before.

King, who is already mom to 16-month-old son James Knight, announced she was expecting her second child with husband Kyle Newman in February.

She also recently revealed why she picked Taylor as her baby-to-be's godmother. "I always thought if I were to have another child that [Taylor] would be the ideal godmother because the way she not only treats me but the way she treats other human beings," King told E! News. "She's so loving and so giving and so generous and she does everything from her heart."

She added, "I know that [our baby] will always have a very good person and role model through her." Too cute!
