Taylor Swift's fashion touchdown! Travis Kelce's No. 1 fan wears racy $695 Daisy Dukes and high heel

Taylor Swift stormed the MetLife Stadium on Sunday while wearing bedazzled Daisy Dukes and sleek boots as she cheered on her new lover and Kansas City Chiefs tight end, Travis Kelce. 

The Cruel Summer hitmaker, 33 - who was spotted with her A-list entourage at the anticipated game against the New York Jets - was seen excitedly supporting the NFL star, also 33, with her pals close by.

She put on a stylish display as she arrived to the stadium located in East Rutherford and donned a long-sleeved, plain black shirt that was tucked into the waist of high-waisted shorts.

The $695 shorts- from the brand called Area - contained sparkling, rhinestone embellishments on the front and the right pocket for a unique flare. 

Throughout the night, Swift also threw on a black, leather jacket to help her stay warm in the cooler, fall temperatures.

She completed her overall ensemble by slipping into a pair of black, knee-high boots to coincide with the color scheme of her fashionable outfit.
