Teyana Taylor Fade 2 Fitness Workout

When Kanye West debuted his "Fade" music video at the MTV VMA's back in August, it wasn't the rapper himself who caused an internet frenzy, but instead Teyana Taylor and her insanely amazing body that stole the show.

Taylor stunned everyone with a hot and sweaty Flashdance-inspired routine, which was filmed in an empty gym. Jaws dropped, gifs were shared, memes were created, gym memberships were bought and the ubiquitous question, "how do I get a body like that?" flooded the internet. If you, like me, still occasionally watch Taylor in the "Fade" video 4 or 5 times in a row (or just on repeat), then you're in luck: the workout video to follow is finally here.

Giphy

Taylor took to Instagram to announce the news of her new dance workout videos, appropriately named "Fade 2 Fit." The Fade2Fit.com site currently has a signup for the upcoming fitness program and a dance fitness tour with a message from Taylor reading: "Everyone keeps asking me what I did to get my body. If you want to know my secret, sign up to get more information on the upcoming dance fitness program and dance workout tour!"

Gentle reminder that the woman in the above video just gave birth to a child not even a full year ago. Please excuse me while I put down the pint of ice cream and sit patiently in front of my screen waiting for the first workout to arrive.
