Man 'arrested for having THREESOME with both of his dogs'

Anthony Danner, 55, has been charged for romping with his female husky called Velvet and a male malamute named Wolf, the Journal Star newspaper reported.

He was arrested on Tuesday following an investigation into the bizarre sex acts that allegedly happened between March 1 and 17.

The 55-year-old's criminal history reveals seven citations for failure to vaccinate his pets for rabies.

Danner, of West Antoinette Street, Illinois, faces two Class four felony counts of sex with an animal.

If found guilty, the dog owner could spend up to three years behind bars.

The court may also ban him from living at home with pets in case it starts again.

He is being held at the Peoria County Jail on a $35,000 bond, local reports said.
