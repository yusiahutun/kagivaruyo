Video: Alan Jackson Is Secretly A Badass

Joe Bielawa from MInneapolis, USA [CC BY 2.0], via Wikimedia Commons

NASHVILLE (CelebrityAccess) Taste of Country is producing a video series on “The Secret History of Country Music” and the segment that came out today makes a compelling argument that Alan Jackson has a lot more outlaw in him than his blond-haired clean-cut image may imply.

Although the segment may try too hard to find examples, such as a tight-lipped conversation between Jackson and George Strait, it does make clear that one does not tell Jackson what to play on awards shows, force him to adhere to radio-friendly tunes and, especially, force him to lip-sync.
