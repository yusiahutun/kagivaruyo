The 15 best cashmere jumpers to add to your forever wardrobe

We earn a commission for products purchased through some links in this article.

Raimonda Kulikauskiene//Getty Images

Begg & Co

Begg x Co's timeless Joy sweater comes in a whole host of versatile hues, so there's bound to be something that'll complement your go-to looks. The crew neckline is comfortably wide, while the shoulders are dropped just enough to create that casual, cocooning fit.

Theory

Short-sleeved and super-fine, Theory's 'feather' cashmere knit is an elevated staple for springtime days. It's woven from pure, 1-ply threads to ensure an ultra-breathable handle, and fits just like your favourite T-shirt.

Arch4

Sporty open collars are everywhere this season, and we can see why: the laid-back silhouette is ideal for smart-casual days. From traceable cashmere brand Arch4, this ivory sweater is best styled with l0w-slung, loose-legged trousers for an effortless working-week look.

Reformation

Reformation

Now 30% Off

There's barely an A-lister that hasn't been snapped in Reformation's coveted cashmere. This cardigan is long, loose and perfect for off-duty days: simply slip it over a white T-shirt for an instant, easy look.

Really Wild

Really Wild

Really Wild might call the corner of Sloane Square home, but the brand's timeless designs are created with country weekends in mind. In a soft shade of taupe, this fine-spun cashmere sweater – which fits slim through the body with slender trims – will work perfectly with a heritage check coat and knee-high boots.

Everlane

Everlane’s jumper is made from a combination of virgin and recycled cashmere, which means the brand is able to cut its carbon emissions while still supporting herder communities. The label is master of crafting simple pieces flawlessly, and this seasonless staple is no exception: expect a chunky handle and boxy fit, with wide ribbed trims for a neat finish.

Jigsaw

Jigsaw

Now 44% Off

Jigsaw's cosy cashmere jumper does come in classic black, but we're drawn in by this mood-boosting fuchsia right now. What better way to infuse a wardrobe of neutrals with a little spring energy?

Allude

Specialist cashmere brand Allude makes some of the best elevated basics around. This classic crew neck is slightly heavier than most – ideal for those who like an extra-cosy knit – and comes in a range of neutral hues, as well as a chic navy and cream stripe. Consider the brand's co-ordinating cashmere tracksuits for an effortless all-in-one look, too.

Uniqlo

The high-street cashmere collection with a cult-like following, Uniqlo’s most prized line contains the perfect everyday sweater. These crew-neck classics come in a wide range of hues, from versatile black to our favourite, duck-egg blue. The handle is a little lighter than most, but the quality stands up to scrutiny: care for yours well, and it’l last for years.

N. Peal’s lightest cashmere option features a fine, breathable handle and a low scoop neck, making it ideal for tucking into trousers.

With cropped sleeves, a tie-fastening hem and cursive embroidered logo, Marni's pure cashmere jumper is a classic with a twist.

Khaite

There's a boyish ease to the slouchy silhouette of Khaite's supremely luxe jumper. The ninety-five per cent cashmere is blended with a hint of elastane to ensure the silhouette stays on the right side of relaxed, while horn buttons along the front ensure a smart finish.

Barrie

A member of Chanel's Métiers D'Art collective, Barrie crafts some of the finest cashmere sweaters in Scotland. This season, they're combining their uncompromising craftsmanship with the bold vision of Sofia Coppola, who has co-designed a collection of colour-blocked sweaters and louche knitted trousers. In short, it's the weekend uniform of dreams.

Skin

We love the twisted detailing on this pure grey cashmere jumper – and if the skin-baring proposition feels at odds with the weather outside, know that it looks equally good layered over a point-collared white shirt.
