Inside Oscar winner Will Smith's jaw-dropping $42m mega-mansion

The Best Actor winner lives in Calabasas with Jada Pinkett Smith

    Will Smith is on a career-high after winning the Oscar for his role in at this year's Academy Awards. His successful career has afforded him a very comfortable life - including an incredible home. 

    The Oscar winner lives in a property worth an estimated $42million with his wife Jada Pinkett-Smith.

    Located in the star-studded neighborhood of Calabasas, which counts the Kardashians among its other famous residents, the estate was fully completed in 2010 and has some seriously impressive facilities, including basketball, volleyball, and tennis courts, its very own lake, and an outdoor pool.

    Inside, the house has nine bedrooms, a meditation lounge, pool room, home theatre, and even a recording studio, where Willow reportedly recorded her hit single .
