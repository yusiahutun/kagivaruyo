William Spetz Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

William Spetz is a Swedish personality and a comedian by profession. He gained attention after he posted YouTube videos on his channel, Lilla Bloggen. Soon, he was hired by the children’s comedy show Williams Lista and later by Scener Ur Ett Tonårsliv. He worked on a Netflix series, Quicksand, in 2019. He will play the titular role in Netflix’s upcoming TV series Tore.  

William Spetz Bio, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth & More

William Spetz was born on 4th April 1996, in Umea, Sweden, where he shared his childhood with his three other siblings, Ebba, Maja, and Saga. His parents are Hakan Spetz and Jenny Markstrom. Other than this, there is no information available about his family members. 

William Spetz Career

William came into the limelight after he started making YouTube videos in the comedy niche. Then, he got an offer from two comedy shows, Williams Lista and Scener ur ett tonårsliv, both aired on SVT Play. When he started blogging at the age of twelve, he did it by the chance of interest.

But, after losing interest and attraction towards his blog post, he asked the paper, Västerbottens-Kuriren, if he could become a youth blogger for their blog portal. Then, he started making vlogs or video blogs on YouTube, “Lilla bloggen” in May 2010.
